[Lyceum Theatre playbill for The Wizard, or, The Brown Man of the Moor, adapted from Sir Walter Scott's novel The Black Dwarf]  Playbill for The Wizard; or The Brown Man of the Moor, also includes advertisements for My Uncle and Batchelors' Wives, among other productions. The musical drama The Wizard, or, The Brown Man of the Moor was adapted from Sir Walter Scott's The Black Dwarf (1816), with words by Samuel James Arnold (1774-1852) and music by Charles Edward Horn (1786-1849). It was first performed at the Lyceum Theatre, London (then known as 'The English Opera House) on 26 July 1817.
