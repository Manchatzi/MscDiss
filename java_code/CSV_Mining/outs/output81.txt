The Citizen and the Soldier = Le Bourgeois et le Soldat Engraving by George Cruikshank of a scene from Scott's novel The Fortunes of Nigel Depicts Captain Colepepper fleeing after his duel with the Jin Vin, the 'citizen of London' (who is glimpsed disappearing through a gate) (Fortunes of Nigel, ch. 12). Landscape-Historical Illustrations of Scotland and the Waverley Novels, vol. I. London: Fisher, Son & Co., 1836-38 (facing p. 3)
