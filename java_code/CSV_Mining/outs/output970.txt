Newgate: As Built by Sir Richard Whittington Engraved title page and frontispiece of Scott's Peveril of the Peak Title page of Peveril of the Peak, volume XXVIII of a set of the Waverley Novels published by A. & C. Black, depicting Newgate Prison, London. In Sir Walter Scott's novel Peveril of the Peak (1823), the hero Julian Peveril is imprisoned in Newgate following an affray with the Duke of Buckingham's men. His father Sir Geoffrey Peveril had earlier been imprisoned in the same gaol on suspicion of involvement in the Popish Plot (1678). The original wood engraving, probably by Ebenezzar Whymper, was made for the Abbotsford Edition of the Waverley Novels (1842-1847). Scott, W. Peveril of the Peak. Edinburgh: Adam and Charles Black, [185-?] London Newgate (Prison)  Popish Plot, 1678
