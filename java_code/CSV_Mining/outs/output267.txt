Dougal Mac Callum and Hutcheon = Dougal Mac Callum & Hutcheon Engraving by George Cruikshank of a scene from Scott's novel Redgauntlet Depicts MacCallum and Hutcheon frightened by an apparition on Sir Robert Regauntlet's coffin (Redgauntlet, letter 11). In this illustration to 'Wandering Willie's Tale', Cruickshank interprets the demonic apparition on Sir Robert Redgauntlet's coffin as a trick of the light involving Sir Robert's pet monkey Landscape-Historical Illustrations of Scotland and the Waverley Novels, vol. I. London: Fisher, Son & Co., 1836-38 (facing p. 15)
