[Covent Garden Theatre playbill for Rob Roy Macgregor, or, Auld Lang Syne, a musical drama adapted from Sir Walter Scott's novel]  Playbill for Rob Roy Macgregor, or Auld Lang Syne; also advertises Who's My Father and Bellamira, or, The Fall of Tunis, among other plays. The musical drama Rob Roy Macgregor, or, Auld Lang Syne was written by Isaac Pocock (1783-1835) with music by Sir Henry Bishop (1786-1855) and John Davy (1763-1824). Adapted from Sir Walter Scott's 1817 novel, it was first performed at Covent Garden, London, on 12 March 1818 and went on to become one of the most popular and frequently revived dramas of the nineteenth century.
