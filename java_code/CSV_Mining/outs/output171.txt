The Mock Battle = La petite guerre Steel engraving by T. A. Prior after a drawing by T. Allom of a scene from Scott's novel Kenilworth Depicts a mock battle between the English and Danes held at Kenilworth Castle, Warwickshire, for Queen Elizabeth's amusement (Kenilworth, ch. 37). Landscape-Historical Illustrations of Scotland and the Waverley Novels, vol. II. London: Fisher, Son & Co., 1836-38 (facing p. 55)   Kenilworth Castle
