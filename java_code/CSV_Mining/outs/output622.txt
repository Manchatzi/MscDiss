No 39, Castle Street, Edinburgh: The Town Residence of Sir Walter Scott for Upwards of Twenty-Five Years. Engraving of No.39 Castle Street, Edinburgh by W. Miller after J.M.W. Turner Depicts Castle Street with Edinburgh Castle in the background. During court term Sir Walter Scott lived with his family at 39 Castle Street, Edinburgh, from 1801 until 1826 when financial disaster compelled Scott to sell up. Lockhart, J.G. Memoirs of the Life of Sir Walter Scott, Bart. Vol. IV. Edinburgh: Robert Cadell. 1839 (t.p.)   Edinburgh
