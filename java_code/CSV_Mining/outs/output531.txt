Lady of the Lake Engraving by F. Engleheart from a drawing by R. Westall illustrating a scene from Scott's poem The Lady of the Lake Depicts Ellen Douglas waving farewell to James Fitz-James with the harper Allan-bane in the foreground (Lady of the Lake, canto II, stanza 6). Illustrates canto II, stanza 6, lines 3-4: 'But when he turned him to the glade, | One courteous parting sign she made.' Scott, W. The Lady of the Lake: Illustrated with Engravings from the Designs of Richd. Westall. London: John Sharpe, 1811 (facing p. 52)
