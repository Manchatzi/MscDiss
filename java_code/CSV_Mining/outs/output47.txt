Philipson and the German Innkeeper = Philipson & l'aubergiste allemand Engraving by George Cruikshank of a scene from Scott's novel Anne of Geierstein Depicts Philipson being presented to the landlord of a German (in fact, Alsatian) inn (Anne of Geierstein, ch. 19). Landscape-Historical Illustrations of Scotland and the Waverley Novels, vol. II. London: Fisher, Son & Co., 1836-38 (facing p. 73)
