Haddon Hall, Derbyshire Engraved title page and frontispiece of Scott's Peveril of the Peak Title page of Peveril of the Peak, volume XXX of a set of the Waverley Novels published by A. & C. Black, depicting Haddon Hall with lit beacon. Martindale Castle, ancient seat of the Peverils in Sir Walter Scott's Peveril of the Peak (1823), is generally thought to be modelled on Haddon Hall, Derbyshire, a fortified medieval manor house belonging to the Manners family. The original wood engraving, probably by Charles Green, was made for the Abbotsford Edition of the Waverley Novels (1842-1847). Scott, W. Peveril of the Peak. Edinburgh: Adam and Charles Black, [185-?]   Haddon Hall
