[Steel engraving by C. Rolls after a drawing by H. Corbould of a scene from Scott's Waverley]  Depicts Alice Bean secretly handing a packet of papers to Waverley (Waverley, ch. 37). This picture was originally commissioned as an illustration to Waverley, ch. 37 but was unpublished. Here it is used to illustrate 'The Highland Cottage', a tale in the manner of Scott by John Carne (1789-1844). Keepsake for MDCCCXXXIII. London: Longman, Rees, Orme & Co., 1832 (facing p. 152)    Jacobite Rebellion, 1745-1746
