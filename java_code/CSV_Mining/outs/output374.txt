Waverley's Last Visit to Flora Mc Ivor Engraving by L. Stocks after a painting by R. Herdman depicting a scene from Scott's novel Waverley Depicts Waverley visiting Flora McIvor, who is sitting at a window with needlework on her lap (Waverley, ch. 68). Eight Engravings in Illustration of Waverley for the Members of the Royal Association for the Promotion of the fine Arts in Scotland. 1865    Jacobite Rebellion, 1745-1746
