Newark Castle on the Yarrow Engraving of Newark Castle by W. Richardson after D.O. Hill Depicts cottages next to the River Yarrow, Scottish Borders, and Newark Castle in the background. In Sir Walter Scott's The Lay of the Last Minstrel (1805), the minstrel receives hospitality from the Duchess of Buccleuch at Newark Castle and, in return, recites a tale about her ancestors. Scott accompanied William Wordsworth on a visit to the ruined 15th-century castle in September 1831 which inspired Wordsworth's poem 'Yarrow Revisited'. Hogg, J. Tales and Sketches by the Ettrick Shepherd. Vol. IV. Glasgow: Blackie & Son, 1837 (front.)   Newark Castle
