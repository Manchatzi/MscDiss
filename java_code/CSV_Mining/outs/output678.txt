East End of Loch Katrine, and Benvenue (Perthshire): Looking South Engraving of Loch Katrine and Ben Venue by J. Swan after J. Fleming Depicts Loch Katrine and Ben Venue. Loch Katrine in Perthshire provides the setting for Sir Walter Scott's The Lady of the Lake (1810). Following the success of the poem, the Loch and the surrounding Trossachs region became major tourist attractions. Leighton, J.M. The Lakes of Scotland: A Series of Views. Glasgow: J.
