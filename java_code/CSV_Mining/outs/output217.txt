[Steel vignette engraving by Charles Heath after a drawing by Richard Westall of a scene from Scott's novel The Monastery]  Shows the White Lady reclaiming her Bible from Father Philip and pitching him into the mill-stream (Monastery, vol. I, ch. VI). Illustrates the lines: ' Landed--landed! the black book hath won. | Else had you seen Berwick with morning sun! | Sain ye, and save ye, and blithe mot ye be, | For seldom they land that go swimming with me.' Scott, W. The Monastery: A Romance by the Author of 'Waverley'. London: Hurst, Robinson & Co., 1821 (t.p.)
