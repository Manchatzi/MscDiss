Mrs. Heukbane & Mrs. Shortcake = Madame Heukbane & Madame Shortcake Engraving by George Cruikshank of characters from Scott's novel The Antiquary Depicts the local gossips Mrs. Heukbane and Mrs. Shortcake examining a love-letter in the Fairport post-office (Antiquary, ch. 15). Landscape-Historical Illustrations of Scotland and the Waverley Novels, vol. I. London: Fisher, Son & Co., 1836-38 (facing p. 27)
