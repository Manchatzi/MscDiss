George of Aspen and Isabella Steel engraving by J. Mitchell from a drawing by F. P. Stephanoff of a scene from Scott's tragedy The House of Aspen Depicts Isabella confessing to George Aspen that she has murdered her first husband (House of Aspen, Act 3, Scene 1). The House of Aspen is an adaptation of Leonhard Waechter's Der heilige Vehme The Keepsake for MDCCCXXX. London: Hurst, Chance & Co., 1829 (facing p. 33)
