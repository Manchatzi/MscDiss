Kenilworth Castle as It Stood in the Reign of Queen Elizabeth, to Illustrate the Romance of Kenilworth, 1575 Facsimile plan of Kenilworth Castle Depicts Kenilworth Castle, Warwickshire, home of the Earl of Leicester in Scott's novel Kenilworth. The now ruined castle Kenilworth Castle was established by Geoffrey de Clinton, Chamberlain to Henry I, in about 1122. This facsimile plan is a copy of a drawing made by William Dugdale in 1656 for his Antiquities of Warwickshire. Account of Kenilworth Castle: With a Key to the Novel of Kenilworth. [Warwick: H. Sharpe, Printer, 182-?] (frontispiece)   Kenilworth Castle
