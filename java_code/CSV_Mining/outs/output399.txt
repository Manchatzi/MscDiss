[Porcelain salt and pepper pots portraying the Scott Monument in Selkirk]  Salt and pepper pots with a design portraying the Scott Monument in Selkirk, Scottish Borders. Sir Walter Scott was Sheriff Depute of Selkirkshire from 1799 until his death in 1832. The monument, with a statue of Scott sculpted by Alexander H. Ritchie, was erected in 1839.
