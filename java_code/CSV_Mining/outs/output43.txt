Catherine Seyton Steel engraving by C. Lewis after a drawing by E. Landseer of a character from Scott's novel The Abbot Depicts Catherine Seyton watching from a window as Roland Graeme rows away from Lochleven Castle, Fife (Abbot, ch. 25). The portrait illustrates the following line: 'As they put off, Roland thought he could discover the face of Catherine Seyton [...], peeping from a loophole to view his departure.' Portraits of the Principal Female Characters in the Waverley Novels: With Illustrative Letter-Press. London: Chapman & Hall, 1833-
