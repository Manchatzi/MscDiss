The Inn at Clovenfords Where Scott Stayed Facsimile of a pencil drawing of Clovenfords Inn by G. Home Depicts Clovenfords Inn, near Galashiels, Scottish Borders. Sir Walter Scott stayed at Clovenfords Inn (now Clovenfords Country Hotel) when fulfilling his duties as Sheriff-Depute of Selkirkshire until 1804 when he moved to Ashestiel. Baikie, J. The Charm of the Scott Country. London: A. & C. Black, 1927 (facing p.35) Clovenfords County Hotel Galashiels
