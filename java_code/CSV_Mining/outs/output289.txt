[Steel engraving by F. F. Walker after a drawing by A. Soloman of a scene from Scott's novel St. Ronan's Well]  Depicts Clara Mowbray rejecting the advances of Valentine Bulmer (St. Ronan's Well, ch. 24). Illustrates the text: '"No nearer--no nearer," she exclaimed, "if you would have me look upon you and live!" Lord Etherington remained standing, as if uncertain whether to advance or retreat, while with incredible rapidity she poured out her hurried entreaties that he would begone.' Scott, W. Waverley Novels. Library Edition, vol. 17, Edinburgh: Adam & Charles Black, 1877
