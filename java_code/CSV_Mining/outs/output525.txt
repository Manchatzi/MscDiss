Marmion Engraving by C. Heath from a drawing by R. Westall illustrating a scene from Scott's poem Marmion Depicts Constance de Beverley on trial before the Abbot of St. Cuthbert and the Abbess of St. Hilda (Marmion, canto II, stanza 21). Illustrates canto II, stanza 21, lines 5-6: 'Her look composed, and steady eye, | Bespoke a matchless constancy'. Scott, W. Marmion: Illustrated with Engravings from the Designs of Richard Westall. London: John Sharpe, 1809 (facing p. 100)    Flodden, Battle of, England, 1513
