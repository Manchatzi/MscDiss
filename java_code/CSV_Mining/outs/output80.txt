The Fair Maid of Perth & Carthusian Monk = La Belle de Perth & le moine carthusien Steel engraving by P. Lightfoot after a painting by A. Chisolm of two characters from Scott's novel The Fair Maid of Perth Depicts a Carthusian monk teaching Catharine Glover, the 'Fair Maid of Perth' with a view of the Tay Valley from Kinoull Hill, 'one of the most beautiful prospects in Scotland' (Fair Maid of Perth, ch. 14). Landscape-Historical Illustrations of Scotland and the Waverley Novels, vol. I. London: Fisher, Son & Co., 1836-38 (facing p. 40)   Tay Valley
