Dirk Hatteraick pursued by the Sloop of War = Dirk Hatteraick poursuivi par le sloop de guerre Steel engraving by James Bayliss Allen after a drawing by G. Balmer of a scene from Scott's novel Guy Mannering Depicts the smuggler Dirk Hatteraick's lugger being pursued by a sloop of war (Guy Mannering, ch. 9). Landscape-Historical Illustrations of Scotland and the Waverley Novels, vol. II. London: Fisher, Son & Co., 1836-38 (facing p. 65)
