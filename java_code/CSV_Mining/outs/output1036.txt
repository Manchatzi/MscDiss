Sir Walter Scott: From a Sketch by Sir Edwin Landseer Pencil drawing of Sir Walter Scott after E. Landseer Pencil sketch of three-quarter portrait of Sir Walter Scott, with, below, a further profile of Scott. The original sketch by Sir Edwin Landseer was probably made during his visit to Abbotsford in 1824. It is now held by the Victoria and Albert Museum. This pencil drawing was probably made by James Clarkson Corson, copied from a reprint of the original sketch in Richard Garnett and Edmund Gosse's English Literature (1903). Gosse, E. English Literature: An Illustrated Record, vol. IV. London: W. Heinemann. 1903
