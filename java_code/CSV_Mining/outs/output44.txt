'He is gone for ever.' = 'Il est parti pour toujours.' Steel engraving by G. Presbury after a painting by F. W. Topham of a scene from Scott's novel The Betrothed Depicts Eveline Berenger on the battlements of Garde Doloureuse, mourning the death of her father Raymond Berenger and being comforted by Rose Flammock (Betrothed, ch. 9). Landscape-Historical Illustrations of Scotland and the Waverley Novels, vol. II. London: Fisher, Son & Co., 1836-38 (facing p. 63)
