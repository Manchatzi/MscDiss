Annot Lyle Coloured lithograph after a drawing by M. O'Connor of the heroine of Scott's novel A Legend of Montrose Depicts Annot Lyle singing and accompanying herself on the harp (Legend of Montrose, ch. 9). The lithograph was made by M. O'Connor from his own design. Scott Gallery of Portraits (The), no. 5. London: I. Pewtrees: Ackermann & Co, 1833
