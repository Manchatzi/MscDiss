Lady of the Lake Engraving by J. Romney from a drawing by R. Westall illustrating a scene from Scott's poem The Lady of the Lake Depicts Brian the Hermit brandishing the Cross of Fire, with Roderick Dhu at his side (Lady of the Lake, canto III, stanza 10). Illustrates canto II, stanza 10, lines 10-11: 'Woe to the wretch who fails to rear | At this dread sign the ready spear!' Scott, W. The Lady of the Lake: Illustrated with Engravings from the Designs of Richd. Westall. London: John Sharpe, 1811 (facing p. 110)
