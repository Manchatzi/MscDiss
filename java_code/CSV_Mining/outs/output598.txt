Drumlanrig House, Dumfriesshire Engraving of Drumlanrig Castle by Angus after F. Grose Engraving of Drumlanrig Castle, Dumfriesshire, and grounds. Scott and his family were frequent visitors at Drumlanrig Castle, seat of the Duke of Buccleuch and Queensberry, Scott's clan-chief and patron. The table in the dining room at Abbotsford was made from oak wood from the grounds of Drumlanrig Castle. This engraving was probably made by William Angus (1752-1821). The original drawing by Francis Grose was made before 1791. Forsyth, R. The Beauties of Scotland. Vol. II. Edinburgh: Printed for Thomson Bonar and John Brown, 1805   Drumlanrig Castle
