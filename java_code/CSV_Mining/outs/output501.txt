Franklin: From an Original picture by J. A. [sic] Duplessis in the Possession of Mr. Barnet Consul General for the United States of America at Paris Steel engraved portrait of Benjamin Franklin by J. Thomson after a painting by J. A. Duplessis Engraved portrait of Benjamin Franklin (1706-1790), American author, publisher, scientist, inventor, and diplomat. Walter Scott quotes from the maxims of Benjamin Franklin in letters to Alexander Ballantyne (24 April 1817) and Daniel Terry (18 February 1824). The original portrait by Duplessis was painted in 1778. The Portrait Gallery of Distinguished Poets, vol. II. London: Wm. S. Orr & Co., 1853
