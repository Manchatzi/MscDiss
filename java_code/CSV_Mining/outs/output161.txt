Wayland Smith's Cave = Caverne de Wayland Smith Steel engraving by R. Staines after a drawing by H. Melville of a scene from Scott's novel Kenilworth Depicts Wayland Smith threatening Tressilian at the mouth of his cave while Dick Sludge attempts to intervene (Kenilworth, ch. 9). Landscape-Historical Illustrations of Scotland and the Waverley Novels, vol. II. London: Fisher, Son & Co., 1836-38 (facing p. 56)
