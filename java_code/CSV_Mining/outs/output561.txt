Sir Walter Scott, Bart Portrait of Sir Walter Scott from an engraving by W. Humphrys after Sir T. Lawrence Three-quarter length portrait of Sir Walter Scott at the age of fifty-five. Scott first sat for the original portrait by Sir Thomas Lawrence in March 1821 (not 1820). Further sittings followed in November 1826, but the painting was not ready for delivery until November 1828. Commissioned by George IV, it is part of the collection at Windsor Castle. The engraving by William Humphrys (not Humphrey) was first published by Graves & Co. in 1844. This is a fascimile of a drawing made from the engraving by an unknown artist. Scott, W. Ivanhoe. London: Daily Express Publications, 1933 (front.)
