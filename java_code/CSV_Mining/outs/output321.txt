Chalon of a character from Scott's novel Woodstock Depicts Alice Lee reading a prayer book, as glimpsed by Everard in Woodstock, ch. 13. Illustrates the text: 'The beautiful and high-born young lady had now approached as near as possible to the brown stuff dress of an ordinary village maiden; but what she had lost in gaiety of appearance, she had gained, as it seemed, in dignity.'
