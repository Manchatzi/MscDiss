York Minster: Moon Light View Engraving of York Minster by W. Miller after C. Stanfield Depicts York Minster in the moonlight. At the conclusion of Sir Walter Scott's novel Ivanhoe (1819), Wilfred of Ivanhoe marries Rowena 'in the most august of temples, the noble Minster of York' (ch. 44). The original steel engraving was made for the Abbotsford Edition of the Waverley Novels (1842-1847). Scott, W. Ivanhoe. Edinburgh: Adam and Charles Black, [185-?]   York Minster
