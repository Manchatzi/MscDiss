The Links of Eyemouth Steel engraving by Edward Finden after a drawing by Copley Fielding of a scene relating to Scott's novel The Bride of Lammermoor Depicts Eyemouth Links, Berwickshire, portrayed in the final scene of The Bride of Lammermoor as the links to the east of Wolf's Hope (ch. 35). The drawing by Copley Fielding was made after a sketch by James Skene Landscape Illustrations of the Waverley Novels: With Descriptions of the Views, vol. I. London: Charles Tilt, 1832 (facing p. 34)   Eyemouth
