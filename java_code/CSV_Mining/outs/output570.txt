Walter Scott's Introduction to Percy's Reliques Facsimile of a portrait of Sir Walter Scott as a child reading in a garden by W. Matthews Portrait of Sir Walter Scott as an eleven-year-old child, reading under a tree in a walled garden. The original portrait was painted in 1914. It depicts Scott in his Aunt Jenny's garden in Kelso, Scottish Borders, in the spring or summer of 1783. The artist is probably Winifred Matthews (born 1886). Cruse, A. Sir Walter Scott. London: George G. Harrap & Co, 1915 (facing p.32)
