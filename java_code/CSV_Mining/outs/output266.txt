Green Mantle Steel engraving by J. Thomson after a drawing by E. T. Parris of a character from Scott's novel Redgauntlet Depicts Lilias Redgauntlet in her green mantle, warning Darsie Latimer of danger (Redgauntlet, Letter 12). Illustrates the text: 'When she entered (wonder if thou will) she wore a green mantle such as thou hast described as the garb of thy fair client. There was an alteration on her brow the instant she recognized me.' (Redgauntlet, Letter 12) Portraits of the Principal Female Characters in the Waverley Novels. London: Charles Tilt, 1834 (p. 28)
