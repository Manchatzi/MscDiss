Inverlochy Castle Steel engraving by E. Finden after a drawing by G. F. Robson of a scene relating to Scott's novel A Legend of Montrose Depicts Inverlochy Castle, Invernessshire, and surrounding landscape. The Battle of Inverlochy (1645), portrayed in the Legend of Montrose (ch. 18), saw Montrose's Royalist forces defeat a Covenanting army.
