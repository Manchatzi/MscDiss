Benmore, Perthshire Lithograph of Ben More by T. Picken after W. L. Leitch Depicts Ben More (or Benmore), Stirling, and surrounding mountains with a cowherd and his cattle in the foreground. The 'proud crest of proud Benmore' is described in one of Scott's earliest poems, Glenfinlas, or Lord Ronald's Coronach (1800). There are further references in Waverley (1814), The Lord of the Isles (1815), and The Fair Maid of Perth (1828). Lawson, J.P. Scotland Delineated: In a Series of Views by Clarkson Stanfield. London: J. Hogarth, 1847-1854   Ben More
