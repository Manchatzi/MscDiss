[Covent Garden Theatre playbill for The Heart of Midlothian, a musical drama adapted from Sir Walter Scott's novel]  Playbill for The Heart of Midlothian, also advertising Fortunatus and his Sons, among other productions. The Heart of Midlothian was a musical drama adapted from Sir Walter Scott's 1818 novel with words by Daniel Terry (1789-1829) and music by Sir Henry Bishop (1786-1855). It was first performed at the Covent Garden Theatre, London, on 17 April 1819.
