Catherine: From a Print by C. Watson after a Picture by Rosselin Steel engraved portrait of Catherine the Great by R. Woodman after a painting by A. Roslin Engraved portrait of Catherine the Great, empress of Russia (1729-1796). Catherine's foreign policy is discussed in Sir Walter Scott's The Life of Napoleon Buonaparte (1827-28), particularly with respect to the partition of Poland and the principle of 'armed neutrality'. The original portrait was painted by Alexander Roslin in 1777-78, and the print by Caroline Watson was published in 1788. The Portrait Gallery of Distinguished Poets, vol. III. London: Wm. S.
