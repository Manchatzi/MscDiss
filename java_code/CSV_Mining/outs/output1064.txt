The Pirate Engraved title page for The Pirate by Freebairn after A. Cooper Engraved title page for The Pirate, vol. XXV in the Magnum Edition of the Waverley Novels. The illustration depicts the heroine Minna Troil riding to Ull Troil's tower. The inscription reads: 'She could not help looking back to see how Minna should pass the point of peril, which she herself had just rounded: Minna, with an eager look dropped the bridle, and stretched forward her arms, and even her body, over the precipice in the attitude of a wild swan, when balancing itself.' (ch. 27) Scott, W. Waverley Novels, vol. XXV. Edinburgh: Cadell & Co, 1831
