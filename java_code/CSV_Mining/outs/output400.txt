[Wooden calling card box decorated with an image of Sir John Steell's statue of Sir Walter Scott]  Calling card box made from elm from Rhymers Glen, Abbotsford, Scottish Borders, with an image of Sir John Steell's statue of Sir Walter Scott. Steell's statue of Scott is part of the Scott Monument in Princes St, Edinburgh, which was inaugurated 15 August 1846.    Abbotsford
