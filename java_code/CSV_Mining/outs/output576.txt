Sir Walter Scott, Bart. Engraved portrait of Sir Walter Scott by J. Horsburgh after J.W. Gordon Three-quarter length portrait of Sir Walter Scott at the age of fifty-eight. Scott is accompanied by his dog Maida. Scott sat for the original portrait by Sir John Watson Gordon in March 1830. Painted for Scott's publisher Robert Cadell, it is now in the possession of the Viscount Cowdray at Dunecht House. The engraving by Horsburgh was first published in 1831 as one of A New Series of Original Illustrations to All Editions of the Waverley Novels (London: Moon, Boys, and Graves). Lockhart, J.G. Memoirs of the Life of Sir Walter Scott, Bart. New Edition, Complete in One Volume. Edinburgh: Robert Cadell, 1845 (front.)
