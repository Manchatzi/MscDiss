Yours truly Sir Walter Scott Facsimile of a sketch by D. Maclise of Sir Walter Scott and two dogs Full-length portrait of Sir Walter Scott aged fifty-four standing in left profile accompanied by two dogs. Below the portrait there is a facsimile of his signature. The original sketch by Daniel Maclise was made on 9 August 1825. It later formed one of a series of portraits published in Fraser's Magazine, 1830-38, under the title 'A Gallery of Illustrious Literary Characters'. Borland, W.P. (ed). The Scott Book: Scenes From the Novels of Sir Walter Scott. London: Bell, 1924 (p.viii)
