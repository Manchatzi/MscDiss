Great Hall, Craignethan Castle: Tillietudlem Steel engraving by J. Goodyear after a drawing by G. Cattermole of a scene from Scott's novel Old Mortality Depicts the great hall of Tillietudlem, with Edith and Lady Bellenden looking on as Major Bellenden asks Claverhouse to spare Morton's life (Old Mortality, ch. 12). The engraving is based on the great hall of Craignethan Castle, the reputed model for Scott's Tillietudlem. Ritchie, L. Scott and Scotland. London: Longman & Co., 1835 (facing p. 254)
