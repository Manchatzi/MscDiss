Ground Plan of the House in which Walter Scott Was Born: From Edgar's Plan of Edinburgh, Drawn in 1742 Facsimile of a map of Edinburgh showing Sir Walter Scott's birthplace Depicts a portion of a plan of Edinburgh showing College Wynd, the birthplace of Sir Walter Scott. Sir Walter Scott was born on 15 August 1771 in a house (since demolished) in College Wynd, Edinburgh. His family moved into new, healthier accommodation in George Square in 1774. Lockhart, C.S.M. The Centenary Memorial of Sir Walter Scott, Bart. London: Virtue, 1871 (facing p.1)   Edinburgh
