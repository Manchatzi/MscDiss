'Julia Mannering' from Sir Walter Scott's 'Guy Mannering' Facsimile of painting of Julia Mannering by Sir J.D. Linton Profile portrait of Julia Mannering, heroine of Scott's novel Guy Mannering. Julia Mannering is the daughter of Guy Mannering in the novel of that name (1815). She marries Harry Bertram, the hero of the tale. Profeit, R.A. Under Lochnagar. Aberdeen: Taylor and Henderson, 1894 (p. 92)
