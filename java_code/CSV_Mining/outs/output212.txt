Tower of Glendearg = Tour de Glendearg Steel engraving by G. Presbury after a drawing by H. Melville of a scene from Scott's novel The Monastery Depicts the Abbot of Kennaqhair with his attendants, accompanied by Sir Piercie Shafton, approaching Glendearg Tower (Monastery, ch. 15). Glendearg Tower is reputedly based on Hillslap Tower, near Galashiels, which is now sometimes known as Glendearg Tower in homage to Scott. Landscape-Historical Illustrations of Scotland and the Waverley Novels, vol. I. London: Fisher, Son & Co., 1836-38 (facing p. 44)   Hillslap Tower
