Peveril of the Peak: Holm-Peel Steel vignette engraving by E. Finden after a drawing by A. Naysmith of a scene relating to Scott's novel Peveril of the Peak Depicts Holm-Peel or Peel Castle, Isle of Man, as featured in Peveril of the Peak, ch. 15. Peel Castle or Holm-Peel figures prominently in Sir Walter Scott's Peveril of the Peak as the residence of the Countess of Derby. The Waverley Album.
