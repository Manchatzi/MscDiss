Jeanie Deans Coloured lithograph after a painting by M. O'Connor of the heroine of Scott's novel The Heart of Midlothian Depicts Jeanie Deans, heroine of Scott's novel The Heart of Mid-Lothian. The lithograph was made by M. O'Connor from his own design. Scott Gallery of Portraits (The), no. 4. London: I. Pewtrees: Ackermann & Co, 1833
