The Forth at Queen's Ferry Steel engraving by J. Kernot after a drawing by G. Cattermole of a scene from Scott's novel The Abbot Depicts armed men in a boat crossing the Firth of Forth at South Queensferry, West Lothian. The men are possibly the delegation sent by the Lords of the Congregation to force Mary Stuart to abdicate (Abbot, ch. 20). Ritchie, L. Scott and Scotland.
