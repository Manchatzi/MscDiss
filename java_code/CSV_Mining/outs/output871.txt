[Engraving of Loch Leven Castle by W. Miller after W. Brown]  Depicts Loch Leven Castle on Castle Island, Loch Leven, Fife. Sir Walter Scott's The Abbot (1820) centres around the imprisonment and escape from Loch Leven Castle of Mary, Queen of Scots. The thirteenth-century castle, built on the largest island in the Loch, also figures repeatedly in Tales of a Grandfather, First Series (1828).    Loch Leven Castle
