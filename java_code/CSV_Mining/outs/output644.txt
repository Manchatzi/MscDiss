The Popping Stone, Gilsland Lithograph of 'The Popping Stone', Gilsland by W.H. McFarlane Depicts a large stone with hawthorn trees in the background. According to local tradition, the 'Popping Stone' in Gilsland, Cumbria, is where Sir Walter Scott proposed to his future wife, Margaret Charlotte Carpenter and the 'Kissing-Thorn' the site of their first kiss. The lithograph is taken from an uncredited photograph. Lockhart, C.S.M. The Centenary Memorial of Sir Walter Scott, Bart. London: Virtue, 1871 (facing p. 20)   Gilsland
