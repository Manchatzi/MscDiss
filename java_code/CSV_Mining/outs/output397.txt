[Two bars of soap with images of the Scott Monument and Edinburgh Castle]  Two bars of soap with images of the Scott Monument (Edinburgh) and Edinburgh Castle. George Meikle Kemp won the competition to design a monument to Sir Walter Scott in Edinburgh in 1838. The structure was begun in 1840 and completed in 1844. The monument, including the a statue of Scott by Sir John Steell, was officially inaugurated 15 August 1846.  Edinburgh Scott Monument
