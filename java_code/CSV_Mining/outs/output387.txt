[Lead toy knight in armour representing figure from MGM film of Scott's Ivanhoe]  A lead toy knight representing Ralph de Vipont, as played by Roderick Lovell, in the 1952 Metro-Goldwyn-Mayer film Ivanhoe, which was based on Scott's novel of the same name. Sir Ralph de Vipont is one of the knights that joust with the disguised Ivanhoe at the tournament of Ashby-de-la-Zouche (Ivanhoe, ch. 7). In his bout with Ivanhoe, he is 'hurled to the ground with such force, that the blood gushed from his nose and his mouth, and he was borne senseless from the lists'.
