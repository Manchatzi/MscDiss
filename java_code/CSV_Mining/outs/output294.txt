Sir Kenneth and the Baron of Gilsland = Le Chevalier Kenneth et le Baron de Gilsland Steel engraving by F. W. Topham of a scene from Scott's novel The Talisman Shows Sir Kenneth and De Vaux at King Richard's sickbed with Saladin disguised as a doctor (Talisman, ch. 8). The image was both painted and engraved by F. W. Topham. Landscape-Historical Illustrations of Scotland and the Waverley Novels, vol. II. London: Fisher, Son & Co., 1836-38 (facing p. 106)    Crusades - Third, 1189-1192
