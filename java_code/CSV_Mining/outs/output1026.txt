[Engraving of boats on Lake Windermere by E. Goodall after C. Stanfield]  Depicts a ferry crossing Lake Windermere, Cumbria. This engraving illustrates a passage in ch. 16 of Sir Walter Scott's Guy Mannering (1815), where Julia Mannering is serenaded by Henry Bertram on an (unnamed) Westmorland lake. Scott met his wife Charlotte when holidaying in the Lake District in 1797. He made further visits to the region in 1805, 1813, and 1825. The original steel engraving was made for the Abbotsford Edition of the Waverley Novels (1842-1847). Scott, W. Guy Mannering. Edinburgh: Adam and Charles Black, [185-?] Lake District Lake Windermere
