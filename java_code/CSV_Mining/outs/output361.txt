[Engraved frontispiece and title-page of Scott's Tales of a Grandfather, second series, vol. III]  Engraved frontispiece depicting the Scottish statesman Andrew Fletcher of Saltoun (1655-1716), featured in Scott's Tales of a Grandfather, vol. III, second series, ch. 7-8. Also vignette engraving of an Edinburgh scene. The portrait was engraved by Lizars from a painting by William Aikman (probably via an earlier engraving). The vignette was engraved from Lizars's own design. Scott, W. Tales of a Grandfather. Edinburgh: Robert Cadell, 1829, vol. 3 (frontispiece)
