St. Anthony's Chapel Engraving of St. Anthony's Chapel, Edinburgh by an unattributed artist Depicts St. Anthony's Chapel, Holyrood Park, Edinburgh, with Calton Hill in the background. In Sir Walter Scott's The Heart of Midlothian (ch. 15), the heroine Jeanie Deans meets her sister's seducer by moonlight beneath St. Anthony's Chapel. The Chapel is also described in Scott's Provincial Antiquities & Picturesque Scenery of Scotland (1826). Black's Picturesque Tourist of Scotland. Edinburgh: Adam and Charles Black, 1840 Edinburgh St. Anthony's Chapel
