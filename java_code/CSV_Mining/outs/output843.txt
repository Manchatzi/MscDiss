Edinburgh from the Frith of Forth Lithograph of the Firth of Forth and Edinburgh by T. Picken after C. Stanfield Depicts ships in the Firth of Forth with a view of Edinburgh in the background. Sir Walter Scott was born and raised in Edinburgh and worked there, first as an Advocate from 1792 to 1806, then as Clerk of the Court of Session until his retirement in 1830. The city features in many of his works, most notably The Heart of Mid-Lothian (1818). Lawson, J.P. Scotland Delineated: In a Series of Views by Clarkson Stanfield. London: J.
