Edith Bellenden Steel engraving by H. Cook after a drawing by G. Cattermole of a character from Scott's novel Old Mortality Three quarter portrait of Edith Bellenden, heroine of Old Mortality, attending the Festival of the Popinjay (Old Mortality, ch. 2). Illustrates the line: 'Her [i.e. Lady Margaret Bellenden's] grand-daughter, and only earthly care, the fair-haired Edith, [...] appeared beside her aged relative like Spring placed close to Winter.' Portraits of the Principal Female Characters in the Waverley Novels: With Illustrative Letter-Press. London: Chapman & Hall, 1833-
