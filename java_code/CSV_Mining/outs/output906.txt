[Theatre Royal, Edinburgh playbill for Rossini's opera La Donna del Lago, based on Sir Walter Scott's The Lady of the Lake]  Playbill for La Donna del Lago, or, The Lady of the Lake; also advertises Il Fanatico per la Musica, among other productions. La Donna del Lago was the first opera to be based on Sir Walter Scott's work and has proved one of the most successful. It was adapted from Scott's narrative poem The Lady of the Lake (1810) with music by Rossini and a libretto by Andrea Leone Tottola.
