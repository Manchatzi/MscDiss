Old Bridge of Tweed Steel engraving by E. Finden after a drawing by W. Westall of a scene relating to Scott's novel The Monastery Depicts the ruins of a bridge over the river Tweed, near Melrose, Scottish Borders, featured in The Monastery, ch. 5. The bridge no longer exists. Westall's design is based on an original sketch by James Skene Landscape Illustrations of the Waverley Novels: With Descriptions of the Views, vol. II. London: Charles Tilt, 1832 (facing p. 44)   Tweed River
