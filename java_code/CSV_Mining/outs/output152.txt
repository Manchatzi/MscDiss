'One foot nearer & I plunge myself from the precipice' = 'Un pied plus pr̬s et je me plonge dans le pr̩cipice' Steel engraving by J. Goodyear after a drawing by G. Cruikshank of a scene from Scott's novel Ivanhoe Depicts Rebecca threatening to throw herself from the parapet of Torquilstone Castle to escape Brian de Bois Gilbert (Ivanhoe, ch. 24). Landscape-Historical Illustrations of Scotland and the Waverley Novels, vol. I. London: Fisher, Son & Co., 1836-38 (facing p. 35)
