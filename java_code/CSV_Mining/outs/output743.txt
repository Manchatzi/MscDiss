Scone Palace Coloured wood block engraving of Scone Palace by B. Fawcett after A.F. Lydon Depicts Scone Palace, Perthshire, and its grounds. Scone Palace, near Perth, was rebuilt between 1803 and 1812 under the supervision of William Atkinson, the architect whom Sir Walter Scott subsequently employed to create Abbotsford. Morris, F.O. The Ancestral Homes of Britain. London: Bell and Daldy, 1868 (facing p. 83) Abbotsford Scone Palace
