Lady of the Lake Engraving by R. Golding from a drawing by R. Westall illustrating a scene from Scott's poem The Lady of the Lake Depicts Ellen Douglas at the feet of James Fitz-James (aka James V of Scotland) (Lady of the Lake, canto VI, stanza 27). Illustrates canto VI, stanza 27, lines 5-6: 'No word her choking voice commands,-- | She showed the ring'. Scott, W. The Lady of the Lake: Illustrated with Engravings from the Designs of Richd. Westall. London: John Sharpe, 1811 (facing p. 284)
