[Theatre Royal, Edinburgh playbill for Guy Mannering, a musical drama adapted from Sir Walter Scott's novel]  Playbill for Guy Mannering, also advertising a performance of Rob Roy for the following night. The 'national opera' Guy Mannering is probably a version of Guy Mannering, or, The Gypsy's Prophecy, a very successful musical drama adapted from Sir Walter Scott's 1815 novel with words by Daniel Terry (1789-1829) and music by Sir Henry Bishop (1786-1855). Scott himself contributed to the libretto. It was first performed at the Covent Garden Theatre, London, on 12 March 1816.
