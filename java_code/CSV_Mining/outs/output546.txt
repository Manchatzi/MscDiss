Lady Scott Portrait of Charlotte, Lady Scott, engraved by G.B. Shaw after J. Saxon Three-quarter portrait of Walter Scott's wife, Charlotte, nee Carpenter (or Charpentier) (1770-1826) at the age of forty. The original portrait by James Saxon was painted in 1810 and is on display at Abbotsford House. Lockhart, J.G. Memoirs of the Life of Sir Walter Scott, Bart. Edinburgh: A. & C. Black, 1871 (facing p. 79)
