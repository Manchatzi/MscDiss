Mrs. H. Siddons of the Theatre Royal Edinburgh as Amy, Countess of Leicester Title page and frontispiece engraving by E. Mitchell of a scene from a dramatization of Scott's novel Kenilworth Depicts the actress Harriet Siddons as Amy, Countess of Leicester, in a stage adaptation of Sir Walter Scott's novel Kenilworth. Kenilworth, adapted for the stage by Thomas J. Dibdin and Alfred Bunn, was first performed at Covent Garden, London, in March 1821. The print was engraved by Mitchell from his own design. Dramas from the Novels and Tales of the Author of 'Waverley', no. 2. Edinburgh: James L. Huie, 1823
