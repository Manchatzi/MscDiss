The Talisman Waltz: On Melodies in M.W. Balfe's Grand Opera, Arranged by Charles Godfrey Coloured lithograph after a sketch by A. Concanen advertising a musical performance of The Talisman Front cover of a musical setting of Scott's novel The Talisman, depicting Sir Kenneth, the Knight of the Leopard, kneeling before a grotto with the Talisman in his hand. The lithograph was executed by Alfred Concanen from his own design. Balfe's unfinished last opera, The Knight of the Leopard, was first performed posthumously in 1874 in an Italian arrangement entitled Il talismano     Crusades - Third, 1189-1192
