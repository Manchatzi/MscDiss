Sir Walter Scott, Bart. Steel engraving by G.B. Shaw of a statue of Sir Walter Scott by J. Greenshields Engraving of a statue of Sir Walter Scott (aged around sixty) with 'Sic Sedebat' inscribed on the pedestal. The original statue by John Greenshields was commenced in 1831 and completed in 1835. It is now displayed in Parliament House, Edinburgh. The engraving was first published in vol. II of J.G. Lockhart's Memoirs of the Life of Sir Walter Scott, Bart. (Edinburgh: Cadell, 1837). Scott, Sir Walter. Waverley Novels, vol. XXV. Edinburgh: R. Cadell, 1843 (front.)
