The Heart of Mid-Lothian Original pencil drawing by James Skene of the Old Tolbooth in Edinburgh Original pencil drawing by James Skene depicting the Old Tolbooth, the prison known as the "Heart of Midlothian" that stood on Edinburgh's High Street until 1817. This drawing was subsequently etched for Skene's A Series of Sketches of the Existing Localities of the Waverley Novels (Edinburgh: Robert Cadell, 1829).    Edinburgh
