Donizetti First page of the aria Per te d'immenso giubilo from Donizetti's opera Lucia di Lammermoor First page of the text and piano score of an aria from Donizetti's opera Lucia di Lammermoor. Donizetti's opera Lucia di Lammermoor, based on Scott's The Bride of Lammermoor, was first performed in the Teatro San Carlo, Naples, 26 September, 1835. The music publishers Hodson & Addison were in business from 1844 to 1848. Donizetti, G. Per te d'immenso giubilo: Aria, in the Opera of Lucia di Lammermoor. London: Hodson & Addison, [184-?]
