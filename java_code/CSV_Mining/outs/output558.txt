Sir Walter Scott, Bart. Engraved portrait of Sir Walter Scott by T. Woolnoth after J. Graham-Gilbert Three-quarter length portrait of a seated Sir Walter Scott at the age of fifty-seven. Scott sat for the original portrait by John Graham (later Graham-Gilbert) between January and June 1829. It was commissioned by the Royal Society of Edinburgh which still owns the portrait. Scott is depicted wearing the gold chain of the President of the Society. Woolnoth's engraving was first published in 1832 in the National Portrait Gallery of Illustrious and Eminent Personages of the Nineteenth Century (London: Fisher, 1831-32) and in the Imperial Magazine, 2nd ser., vol.
