[Engraved title page of the Abbotsford Edition of the Waverley Novels]  Depicts arms, armaments and other objects from Sir Walter Scott's private collection at Abbotsford or associated with the Waverley Novels. Also depicted are some of Scott's dogs and, in the background, Abbotsford House. Title page of Part LVIII of the Abbotsford Edition of the Waverley Novels, published June 1844, containing chapters 1-6 of The Pirate. The illustration is identical for each part of the serialized edition, and for each volume of the published set. Scott, W. Waverley Novels. Abbotsford Edition. Pt. LVIII. Edinburgh: Robert Cadell, 1844   Abbotsford
