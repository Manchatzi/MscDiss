Hyder Ali Engraved title page and frontispiece of Scott's Surgeon's Daughter Title page of The Surgeon's Daughter, volume XLVIII of a set of the Waverley Novels published by A. & C. Black, depicting Hyder Ali (ca. 1720-1782). In Sir Walter Scott's The Surgeon's Daughter (1827), Haidar (or Hyder Ali), Nawab of Mysore, assures the safety of the heroine, Menie Gray, and punishes her duplicitous fiance Richard Middlemass. The original wood engraving was made for the Abbotsford edition of the Waverley Novels (1842-1847). Scott, W. The Surgeon's Daughter. Edinburgh: Adam and Charles Black, [185-?]
