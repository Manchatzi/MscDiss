Worcester Engraving of Worcester by W. Miller after T. Creswick Depicts the River Severn and city of Worcester. The plot of Sir Walter Scott's novel Woodstock (1826) revolves around the escape of King Charles II following his decisive defeat by Cromwell at the Battle of Worcester (3 September 1651). The original steel engraving was made for the Abbotsford Edition of the Waverley Novels (1842-1847). Scott, W. Woodstock.
