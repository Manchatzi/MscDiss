Dollond: From an Original Picture in the Royal Observatory, Greenwich Steel engraved portrait of Dollond by J. Posselwhite Engraved portrait of the British optician John Dollond (1706-1761). Dollond is best-known as the first person to patent the achromatic doublet. The original portrait is by Benjamin Wilson. The Portrait Gallery of Distinguished Poets, vol. II. London: Wm. S. Orr & Co., 1853
