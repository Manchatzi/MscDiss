[Title page of Sir Walter Scott's Manners, Customs, and History of the Highlanders of Scotland and Historical Account of the Clan MacGregor]  Title page of a volume containing two essays by Scott on Highland history. The first essay was originally published as a review of the Culloden Papers in the January 1816 issue of the Quarterly Review. The second essay was first published as the Introduction to the 'Magnum Opus' edition of Rob Roy (Edinburgh: Cadell, 1830). Scott, W. Manners, Customs and History of the Highlanders of Scotland and Historical Account of the Clan MacGregor. Glasgow: T.D. Morison, 1893   Highlands
