[Engraving of Windsor Castle by J. Horsburgh after T. Creswick]  Depicts Windsor Castle, Berkshire, by moonlight. In Sir Walter Scott's Woodstock (1826), Cromwell is stationed at Windsor while awaiting the capture of the fugitive King Charles II. The original steel engraving was made for the Abbotsford Edition of the Waverley Novels (1842-1847). Scott, W. Woodstock. Edinburgh: Adam and Charles Black, [185-?]   Windsor Castle
