Walter Scott. Surnamed "Beardie", Great-Grandfather of Sir Walter Scott Portrait of Walter Scott of Raeburn engraved by G.B. Shaw Three-quarter portrait of Walter Scott's great-grandfather, Walter Scott, 1st Laird of Raeburn (1653-1729). Walter Scott of Raeburn was known as 'Beardie' having sworn not to shave until the Stuarts were restored to the crown of Scotland. His property was confiscated following his participation in the Jacobite Rebellion of 1715. The painter of the original portrait is unknown. Lockhart, J.G. Memoirs of the Life of Sir Walter Scott, Bart. Edinburgh: A. & C. Black, 1871 (facing p. 20)
