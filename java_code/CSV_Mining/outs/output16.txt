Loch Leven Steel engraving by E.F. Finden from a drawing by H. Gastineau depicting a scene relating to Scott's novel The Abbot Depicts Loch Leven Castle, Fife, scene of Mary Stuart's imprisonment, with washerwomen in the foreground. The plot of Sir Walter Scott's The Abbot (1820) revolves around Mary's escape from the castle. Landscape Illustrations of the Waverley Novels: With Descriptions of the views, vol. II. London: Charles Tilt, 1832 (no. 49)   Loch Leven Castle
