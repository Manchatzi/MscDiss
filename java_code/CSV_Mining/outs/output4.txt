Loch Leven and Castle Steel engraving by W. Miller after C. Stanfield of a scene relating to Scott's novel The Abbot Depicts fishermen on Loch Leven, Fife, with the Castle, scene of Mary Stuart's imprisonment, in the background. The plot of Sir Walter Scott's The Abbot (1820) revolves around Mary's escape from the castle. Scott, W. Waverley Novels, Library Edition, vol. II, The Abbot. Edinburgh: 1877 (facing p. 298)   Loch Leven Castle
