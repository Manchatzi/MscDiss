The Heart of London: From Tower Bridge Facsimile of a painting of London by E.W. Haslehust Depicts a view of London from the Tower Bridge, including the Thames River and St. Paul's Cathedral. Sir Walter Scott visited London on several occasions, and the city appears in a number of his novels, most notably The Fortunes of Nigel (1822) and Peveril of the Peak (1823). Jerrold, W. The Heart of London. London: Blackie and Son, 1924 (front.)   London
