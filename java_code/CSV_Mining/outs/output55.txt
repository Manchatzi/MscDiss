Fielding depicting a scene relating to Scott's novel The Black Dwarf Depicts Manor Valley, Tweeddale, home of the Sir Edward Mauley in Scott's novel The Black Dwarf. The image was drawn by Copley Fielding after an original sketch by Scott's friend James Skene of Rubislaw. Landscape Illustrations of the Waverley Novels: With Descriptions of the Views, vol. I. London: Charles Tilt, 1832 (facing p. 21)   Manor Valley
