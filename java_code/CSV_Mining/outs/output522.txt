Lay of the Last Minstrel Steel engraving by C. Heath from a drawing by R. Westall of a scene from Scott's poem The Lay of the Last Minstrel Depicts the Abbot of Melrose Abbey blessing pilgrims on the day of penitence (Lay of the Last Minstrel, canto VI, stanza 31). Illustrates canto VI, stanza 31 (not stanza 30 as stated), lines 10-11: 'The mitred abbot stretched his hand, | And blessed them as they kneeled'. Scott, W. The Lay of the Last Minstrel: Illustrated with Engravings from the Designs of Richd. Westall. London: John Sharpe, 1809 (facing p. 206)
