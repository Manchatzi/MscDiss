Altrieve Cottage: The Residence of the Ettrick Shepherd Engraving of Altrive Cottage by J. Swan after J. Fleming Depicts Altrieve Cottage, near Ettrick, Selkirkshire, and the attached hill-farm. Altrieve Cottage was the home of poet and novelist James Hogg, known as the 'Ettrick Shepherd'. Sir Walter Scott met Hogg while researching material for Minstrelsy of the Scottish Border and became his lifelong friend and patron. Leighton, J.M. The Lakes of Scotland: A Series of Views. Vol. II.
