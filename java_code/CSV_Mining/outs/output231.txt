A Gallery in Craignethan Castle: Tillietudlem Steel engraving by C. Heath after a drawing by G. Cattermole of a scene from Scott's novel Old Mortality Depicts Jenny Dennison, Edith Bellenden's maid, bribing the guard Tam Halliday to let her mistress see Henry Morton (Old Mortality, ch. 10). The scene is set in a gallery of Craignethan Castle, Lanarkshire, reputedly portrayed in Old Mortality as Tillietudlem. Ritchie, L. Scott and Scotland. London: Longman & Co., 1835 (facing p. 254)
