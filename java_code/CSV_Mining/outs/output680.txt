Loch Leven & Castle (Kinross-shire): Looking West Engraving of Loch Leven Castle by J. Swan after J. Fleming Depicts three figures among the ruins of Loch Leven Castle, Fife. Sir Walter Scott's The Abbot (1820) centres around the imprisonment and escape from Loch Leven Castle of Mary, Queen of Scots. The thirteenth-century castle, built on the largest island in the Loch, also figures repeatedly in Tales of a Grandfather, First Series (1828). Leighton, J.M. The Lakes of Scotland: A Series of Views. Glasgow: J. Swan, 1834 (facing p.207)   Loch Leven Castle
