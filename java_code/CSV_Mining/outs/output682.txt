Loch Lochy (Invernesshire): From Tor-a-cromerk, Looking North East Engraving of Loch Lochy by J. Swan after J. Fleming Depicts a small farmstead on the shores of Loch Lochy, Scottish Highlands. Loch Lochy appears in Sir Walter Scott's A Legend of Montrose (1819), ch. 18, as the Marquis of Argyll's army prepares to set up headquarters in Inverlochy Castle. Leighton, J.M.
