Craignethan Castle, Lanarkshire Engraving of Craignethan Castle by R. Brandard after C. Stanfield Depicts Craignethan Castle, Lanarkshire. Craignethan Castle is generally held to be the inspiration for Tillietudlem Castle, seat of the Bellenden family in Walter Scott's Old Mortality (1816). Scott had visited the ruins of the 16th-century castle in 1799 in the company of Lord and Lady Douglas. The original steel engraving was made for the Abbotsford Edition of the Waverley Novels (1842-1847). Scott, W. Old Mortality. Edinburgh: Adam and Charles Black, [185-?]   Craignethan Castle
