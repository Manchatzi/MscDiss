Kenilworth: Warwick Castle Steel vignette engraving by E. Finden of a scene relating to Scott's novel Kenilworth Vignette depiction of Warwick Castle, Warwickshire, in which Queen Elizabeth stays en route to Kenilworth Castle (Kenilworth, ch. 25). The vignette is presumably engraved after Finden's own design. The Waverley Album. London: Charles Heath, 1832 (facing p. 34)   Warwick Castle
