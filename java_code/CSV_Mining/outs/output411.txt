[Title page of Mary Queen of Scots, or, Loch Leven Castle: A Drama Founded on The Abbot] Illustrated title page of the play Mary Queen of Scots; or, Loch Leven Castle, with an engraving by A. Wilson Depicts Loch Leven Castle near Kinross, Fife, scene of Mary Stuart's imprisonment (The Abbot, ch. 31-35). This adaptation of Scott's novel The Abbot has been attributed to Daniel Terry. The engraving by Wilson is from his own design. Scott. W. Scott's Dramas. Edinburgh: John Anderson, 1825   Loch Leven Castle
