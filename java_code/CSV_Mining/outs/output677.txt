Loch Katrine (Perthshire): From Craiginnes Looking South West Engraving of Loch Katrine by J. Swan after J. Fleming Depicts Loch Katrine, Stirling, in the moonlight. Loch Katrine provides the setting for Sir Walter Scott's The Lady of the Lake (1810). Following the success of the poem, the Loch and the surrounding Trossachs region became major tourist attractions. Leighton, J.M. The Lakes of Scotland: A Series of Views. Glasgow: J.
