Anne of Geierstein Engraved title page for Anne of Geierstein by J. Rolls after J.W. Wright Engraved title page for Anne of Geierstein, vol. XLV in the Magnum Opus Edition of the Waverley Novels. The illustration depicts the heroine's attendant Annette announcing to her the arrival of Arthur de Vere at Castle Arnheim. The inscription reads: 'Annette sped up a narrow turnpike stair to a closet or dressing-room, where her young mistress was seated, and exclaimed with open mouth -- Anne of Gei -- I mean my Lady Baroness, they are come, they are come !' (ch. 21) Scott, W. Waverley Novels, vol. XLV. Edinburgh: Cadell & Co., 1833
