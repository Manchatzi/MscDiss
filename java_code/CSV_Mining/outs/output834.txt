The Doune Lithograph of Doune Castle by T. Picken after J. Dobbin Depicts cattle on the banks of the Teith River with Doune Castle, Stirling, in the background. In ch. 38 of Sir Walter Scott's Waverley (1814), Edward Waverley is rescued by a party of Jacobite Highlanders and escorted to Doune Castle in the Menteith district, a 'gloomy yet picturesque structure'. There are further references to the Castle in other works by Scott, notably The Lady of the Lake, canto VI. Lawson, J.P. Scotland Delineated: In a Series of Views by Clarkson Stanfield. London: J.
