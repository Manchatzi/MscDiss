Dumfries Engraving of Dumfries, Scotland by W. Miller after C. Stanfield Depicts the River Nith and the town of Dumfries. The first part of Sir Walter Scott's Redgauntlet (1824) consists of a series of letters between Darsie Latimer and Alan Fairford. Darsie's first letter is sent from Dumfries which he has made his base while rambling through the countryside of Dumfries and Galloway. The original steel engraving was made for the Abbotsford Edition of the Waverley Novels (1842-1847). Scott, W. Redgauntlet. Edinburgh: Adam and Charles Black, [185-?]   Dumfries
