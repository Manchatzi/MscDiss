Holy Loch Steel engraving by Edward Finden after a design by J. D. Harding of a scene from Scott's novel The Heart of Midlothian Depicts the Holy Loch (Argyll and Bute) on which the fictional town of Knockdunder is situated in Scott's The Heart of Midlothian. The design by J. D. Harding was made after an original sketch by Scott's friend James Skene of Rubislaw. Landscape Illustrations of the Waverley Novels: With Descriptions of the Views, vol. I. London: Charles Tilt, 1832 (facing p. 30)   Holy Loch
