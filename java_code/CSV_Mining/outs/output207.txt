Le Monast̬re Steel engraving by A. Caron after a drawing by A. Johannot of a scene from Scott's novel The Monastery Depicts Sir Piercie Shafton attempting to revive Mary Avenel. Behind him, Edward Glendinning is being prevented from drawing his sword to avenge his brother Halbert whom he wrongly believes Shafton has killed (Monastery, ch. 26).
