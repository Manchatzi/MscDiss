The Castle of Ashby Steel engraving by E. Finden after a drawing by G. Cattermole of a scene relating to Scott's novel Ivanhoe Depicts the ruins of Ashby Castle, Ashby-de-la-Zouch, Leicestershire, scene of the knightly tournament in Ivanhoe, ch. 8. Cattermole's design is based on an original sketch by Scott's friend James Skene of Rubislaw. Landscape Illustrations of the Waverley Novels: With Descriptions of the Views, vol. II. London: Charles Tilt, 1832 (facing p. 40)   Ashby Castle
