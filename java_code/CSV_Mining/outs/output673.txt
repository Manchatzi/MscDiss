Loch Earn (Perthshire): Looking North West Engraving of Loch Earn by J. Swan after J. Fleming Depicts Loch Earn, Scottish Highlands, and surrounding mountains. Much of Sir Walter Scott's A Legend of Montrose (1819) is set on the south bank of Loch Earn in or around the fictional Darlinvarich Castle, which is modelled on Ardvoirlich Castle. The Loch is also mentioned briefly in The Fair Maid of Perth (1828). Leighton, J.M. The Lakes of Scotland: A Series of Views. Glasgow: J. Swan, 1834 (facing p.63)   Loch Earn
