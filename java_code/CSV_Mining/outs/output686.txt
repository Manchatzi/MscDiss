Ben Lomond & Loch Lomond Engraving of Loch Lomond and Ben Lomond by an unattributed artist Depicts Loch Lomond with Ben Lomond in the background. Loch Lomond forms a dramatic part of the scenery in a number of Sir Walter Scott's works, including Rob Roy and The Lady of the Lake, where Roderick Dhu is portrayed as 'the terror of Loch Lomond's side' (canto II, stanza XI, line 14). Scott visited the Loch with his wife in the summers of 1809, 1816, and 1817. Black's Picturesque Tourist of Scotland. Edinburgh: Adam and Charles Black, 1840   Loch Lomond
