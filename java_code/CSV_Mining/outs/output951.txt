Entry to Vault, Arbroath Abbey Engraved title page and frontispiece of Scott's The Antiquary Title page of The Antiquary, volume V of a set of the Waverley Novels published by A. & C. Black, featuring Arbroath Abbey, Angus. St Ruth's Priory, scene of a number of important episodes in Sir Walter Scott's The Antiquary (1816), is often thought to be based on the 12th-century Arbroath Abbey. Scott visited Arbroath in September 1796. The original wood engraving was made for the Abbotsford edition of the Waverley Novels (1842-1847). The artist is Patrick Allan-Fraser who assumed his wife's surname of Fraser on his marriage in 1843. Scott, W. The Antiquary. Edinburgh: Adam and Charles Black, [185-?]   Arbroath Abbey
