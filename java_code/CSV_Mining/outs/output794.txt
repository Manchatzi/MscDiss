Napoleon: From a Picture by F. Gerard in the Possession of the Publisher Engraved portrait of Napoleon Bonaparte by W. Holl after F. Gerard Engraved portrait of Napoleon I (1769-1821) in his coronation robes. Walter Scott published his nine-volume Life of Napoleon Bonaparte in 1827. In a letter to Lady Abercorn (April 21, 1824), he described Napoleon as 'a strange mingled phantom of grandeur and terror and a little meanness withal as ever bestrode the destinies of the world'. The original portrait by Baron Fran̤ois G̩rard was painted in 1805/06. The Portrait Gallery of Distinguished Poets, vol. III. London: Wm. S. Orr & Co., 1853
