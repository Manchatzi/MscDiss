Flodden Field Facsimile of a pencil drawing of Flodden Field by G. Home Depicts a stormy sky over Flodden Field, Northumberland. Sir Walter Scott's poem Marmion culminates in one of the greatest disasters in Scottish history, the Battle of Flodden Field. It is described at length in Canto Sixth ('The Battle'). Scott visited the battlefield in August 1791. Baikie, J. The Charm of the Scott Country. London: A. & C. Black, 1927 (facing p. 16)    Flodden, Battle of, England, 1513
