Sir Walter Scott Engraved profile portrait by E. Lacey from a medal of Sir Walter Scott by A. J. Stothard Depicts a medallion with a profile portrait of Sir Walter Scott (aged around fifty) and a border decorated with military and Scottish motifs. The portrait is engraved by Edward Lacey from a medal cast by A. J. Stothard in 1827. The ornament is by Samuel Mitan after Henry Corbould. Stothard's medal is derived from the bust by Sir Francis Legatt Chantrey. The Waverley Album: Containing Fifty-one Line Engravings to Illustrate the Novels and Tales of Sir Walter Scott. London: Charles Heath. 1832 (frontis)
