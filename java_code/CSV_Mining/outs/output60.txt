Coldingham Steel engraving by Edward Finden after a drawing by Samuel Prout of a scene from Scott's novel The Bride of Lammermoor Depicts the ruins at Coldingham Priory, Berwickshire, scene of Lord Allan's funeral in The Bride of Lammermoor, ch. 2. The drawing by Samuel Prout was made after a sketch by Scott's friend James Skene of Rubislaw. Landscape Illustrations of the Waverley Novels: With Descriptions of the Views, vol I. London: Charles Tilt, 1832 (facing p. 32)   Coldingham Priory
