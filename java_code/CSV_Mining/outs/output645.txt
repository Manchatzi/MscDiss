Glasgow: From the Green Engraving of the city of Glasgow by E. Francis after W. Westall Depicts Glasgow Green and the Clyde River with the city in the background. The city of Glasgow features prominently in Sir Walter Scott's Rob Roy (1817). Francis Osbaldistone crosses (and describes) Glasgow Green in Chapter 21 of the novel. Scott visited Glasgow on several occasions, notably in 1808, 1814, and 1817. Moule, T. The Landscape Album, or, Great Britain Illustrated: In a Series of Sixty Views.
