The Laird's Jock Steel engraving by Charles Heath after a drawing by H. Corbould of a scene from Scott's tale The Death of the Laird's Jock Depicts the Laird's Jock's despair as he sees his son killed in single combat with an English champion, and his daughter attempting to restrain and comfort him. The short story 'Death of the Laird's Jock' was published by Scott in the 1828 issue of The Keepsake. Stevens, H. I. and A. Scott and Scotland. London: H. I. and A. Stevens, 1845
