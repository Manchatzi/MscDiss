The Fortunes of Nigel: The Scene with Nigel, Trapbois, and Colepepper Steel engraving by J. Mitchell after a drawing by J. M. Wright of a scene from Scott's novel The Fortunes of Nigel Shows the usurer Trapbois preventing Nigel Olifaunt and Captain Colepepper from coming to blows (Fortunes of Nigel, ch. 23).
