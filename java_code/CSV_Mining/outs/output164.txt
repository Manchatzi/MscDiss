Dickie Sludge and the Giant Porter = Dickie Sludge et le grand portier. Engraving by George Cruikshank of a scene from Scott's novel Kenilworth Shows the giant porter holding Dickie Sludge high in the air in front of the entrance to Kenilworth Castle, Warwickshire (Kenilworth, ch. 26). Landscape-Historical Illustrations of Scotland and the Waverley Novels, vol. I. London: Fisher, Son & Co., 1836-38 (facing p. 54)   Kenilworth Castle
