Highland Hills, From the Teith Below Callander Engraving of the Highland Hills, near Callander by W. Miller after C. Stanfield Depicts a view from the River Teith, Perthshire. In ch. 38 of Sir Walter Scott's Waverley (1814), Edward Waverley is rescued by a party of Jacobite Highlanders and escorted through the Menteith district and over the River Teith to Doune Castle. The original steel engraving was made for the Abbotsford Edition of the Waverley Novels (1842-1847). Scott, W. Waverley. Edinburgh: Adam and Charles Black, [185-?]   Teith River
