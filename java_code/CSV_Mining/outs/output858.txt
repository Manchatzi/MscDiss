Flodden Pencil drawing tinted with watercolour of Flodden Field by P. Home Depicts Flodden Field, Northumberland, and surrounding countryside. Sir Walter Scott's poem Marmion culminates in one of the greatest disasters in Scottish history, the Battle of Flodden Field. It is described at length in Canto Sixth ('The Battle'). Scott visited the battlefield in August 1791.     Flodden, Battle of, England, 1513
