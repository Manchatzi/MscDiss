Newark Castle: On the Yarrow Engraving of Newark Castle by an unattributed artist Depicts Newark Castle, Scottish Borders, overlooking the River Yarrow. In Sir Walter Scott's The Lay of the Last Minstrel (1805), the minstrel receives hospitality from the Duchess of Buccleuch at Newark Castle and, in return, recites a tale about her ancestors. Scott accompanied William Wordsworth on a visit to the ruined 15th-century castle in September 1831 which inspired Wordsworth's poem 'Yarrow Revisited'.    Newark Castle
