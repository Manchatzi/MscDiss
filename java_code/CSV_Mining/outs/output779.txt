Author of 'Memoirs of Illustrious Personages' Facsimile reproduction of a print portrait of Edmund Lodge by Daniel Maclise Facsimile of sketch of English historian Edmund Lodge (1756-1839) dressed in the robes of office of Norroy, King of Arms. Scott admired the work of Edmund Lodge and frequently drew upon his Illustrations of British History (1791). Lodge's Portraits of Illustrious Personages of Great Britain (1821-35) was prefixed by a letter of recommendation from Scott. The twelfth and final volume of Lodge's portraits contained a biographical sketch of Scott. This facsimile reproduction is of one of a series of portraits originally published in Fraser's Magazine, 1830-38, under the title 'A Gallery of Illustrious Literary Characters'. Maclise, D. The Maclise Portrait Gallery of Illustrious Literary Characters, ed. W. Bates. London: Chatto and Windus, 1883 (facing p. 206)
