The Middlemas Wives & Gideon Gray = Les femmes de Middlemas & Gideon Gray Engraving by George Cruikshank of a scene from Scott's story The Surgeon's Daughter Depicts Dr Gideon Gray and a group of Middlemas women who are competing for the right to be Zilia de Moncada's nurse (The Surgeon's Daughter, ch. 1). Landscape-Historical Illustrations of Scotland and the Waverley Novels, vol. II. London: Fisher, Son & Co., 1836-38 (facing p. 81)
