Hermanos, coged al prisionero y quitadle la espada Coloured illustration from a Spanish translation of Scott's novel Old Mortality An illustration from a Spanish abridgement of Old Mortality, depicting Morton being taken prisoner by a band of Cameronians (Old Mortality, ch. 33). The caption is translation of a line spoken by Ephraim Macbriar: 'Seize on the prisoner, brethren, and take his weapon.' Scott, W. Enrique Morton de Milnwood (tr. J.A.). Barcelona: J. Roura, 1893 (facing p. 64)
