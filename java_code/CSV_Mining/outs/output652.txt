The Scott Monument, Edinburgh Facsimile of a pencil drawing by W.J. Maskell of the Scott Monument Depicts the Scott Monument, Edinburgh, with the spire of St Giles' Cathedral visible in the background. George Meikle Kemp won the competition to design a monument to Sir Walter Scott in 1838. The structure was begun in 1840 and completed in 1844. The monument, including the statue by Sir John Steell, was officially inaugurated 15 August 1846.  Edinburgh Scott Monument
