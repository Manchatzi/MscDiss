Newark Tower Facsimile of a painting of Newark Castle by E.W. Haslehust Depicts the River Yarrow, Scottish Borders, with Newark Castle silhouetted in the background. In Sir Walter Scott's The Lay of the Last Minstrel (1805), the minstrel receives hospitality from the Duchess of Buccleuch at Newark Castle and, in return, recites a tale about her ancestors. Scott accompanied William Wordsworth on a visit to the ruined 15th-century castle in September 1831 which inspired Wordsworth's poem 'Yarrow Revisited'. Geddie, J. The Scott Country.
