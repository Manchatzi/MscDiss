Original Design for the Scott Monument Facsimile of the design for the Scott Monument by G.M. Kemp Depicts the original design for the Scott Monument, Edinburgh, including small turrets at the corners. George Meikle Kemp won the competition to design a monument to Sir Walter Scott in Edinburgh in 1838. The structure was begun in 1840 and completed in 1844. The monument, including the statue of Scott by Sir John Steell, was officially inaugurated 15 August 1846. Bonnar, T.
