Roberts of Kelso Abbey Depicts the ruined Abbey of Kelso, Scottish Borders, as described in Scott's Essay on Border Antiquities (1814). Scott stayed in Kelso with his Aunt Janet after leaving school in 1783 and again from 1784-1785 when illness forced him to suspend his studies at Edinburgh University. Lawson, J.P.
