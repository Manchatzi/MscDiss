Caerlaverock Castle Engraved title page and frontispiece of Scott's Guy Mannering Title page of Guy Mannering, volume III of a set of the Waverley Novels published by A. & C. Black, featuring Caerlaverock Castle, Dumfries and Galloway. Ellangowan Castle, seat of the Bertrams in Sir Walter Scott's Guy Mannering (1815), is often thought to be based on Caerlaverock Castle in Dumfriesshire. Scott visited Caerlaverock Castle (built by the Maxwells in the 13th century) with Guthrie Wright in 1806. The original wood engraving was made for the Abbotsford edition of the Waverley Novels (1842-47). Scott, W. Guy Mannering. Edinburgh: Adam and Charles Black, [185-?]   Caerlaverock Castle
