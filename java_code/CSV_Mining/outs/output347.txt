Grave of Helen Walker: "Jeanie Deans" Half-tone reproduction of a photograph by James White of Helen Walker's grave Reproduced photograph of the grave of Helen Walker in Irongray, Wigtownshire. Walker was the prototype for Jeanie Deans in Scott's novel The Heart of Midlothian. Crockett, W.S. The Scott Originals. London, Edinburgh: T.N. Foulis, 1912   Irongray
