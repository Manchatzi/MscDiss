The Battle of Bothwell Bridge Steel engraving by J. B. Allen after a drawing by W. Harvey of a scene relating to Scott's novel Old Mortality Depicts the government and Covenanting armies meeting at the Battle of Bothwell Bridge, June 22, 1679. The battle is depicted in Old Mortality, ch. 31-32. Landscape-Historical Illustrations of Scotland and the Waverley Novels, vol. I.
