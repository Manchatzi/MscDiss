Legend of Montrose: Old Inverary Steel vignette engraving by Charles Heath from a drawing by P. de Wint of a setting for Scott's novel A Legend of Montrose Vignette engraving showing Inverary Castle, seat of the Dukes of Argyll, on the shores of Loch Fyne (Argyll and Bute).
