[Steel engraving by C. Rolls after a drawing by Henry James Richter of a scene from Scott's novel Old Mortality]  Depicts John Balfour of Burley showing Henry Morton the notches on his sword (Old Mortality, ch. 43). Originally commissioned as an illustration to the Waverley Novels, but unpublished, this engraving was subsequently used to illustrate the tale 'The Novice' in W. H. Harrison's Christmas Tales (1833). Harrison, W. H. Christmas Tales, Historical and Domestic. London: Jennings & Chaplin, 1833 (facing p. 164)
