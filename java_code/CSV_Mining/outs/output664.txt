Lancaster Castle Engraving of Lancaster Castle by R. Parr after W. Westall Depicts the John O' Gaunt Gateway, Lancaster Castle, Lancashire. Lancaster Castle was founded by Roger of Poitou at the end of the eleventh century. Sir Walter Scott, in his Letters on Demonology and Witchcraft, describes the Lancashire witch trials of 1612 (Letter VIII) where ten women were convicted of witchcraft at Lancaster Castle and hanged.
