Libberton's Wynd, Edinburgh Lithograph of Liberton's Wynd, Edinburgh by T. Picken after G. Cattermole Depicts street life on Libberton's Wynd, Edinburgh, outside Burn's Tavern. From 1817 until the date of the last public execution, the head of Libberton's (or Liberton's) Wynd, Edinburgh, was the site of the gallows. Among other executions, Sir Walter Scott witnessed that of the body-snatcher and murderer William Burke, 28 January, 1829. Lawson, J.P. Scotland Delineated: In a Series of Views by Clarkson Stanfield. London: J. Hogarth, 1847-1854   Edinburgh
