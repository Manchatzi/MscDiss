Cottage in Ettrick: The Birth Place of James Hogg Engraving of Ettrick Cottage by D. Wilson after D.O. Hill Depicts Ettrick Cottage, Selkirkshire, with horse and cart on the road outside. The engraving shows the birthplace of novelist and poet James Hogg, known as the Ettrick Shepherd. Sir Walter Scott met Hogg while researching material for The Minstrelsy of the Scottish Border and became his lifelong friend and patron. Hogg, J. Tales and Sketches by the Ettrick Shepherd. Vol. V. Glasgow: Blackie & son, 1837 (front.)   Ettrick and Lauderdale
