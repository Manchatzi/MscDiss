[Facsimile of silhouette of Walter Scott at his desk by A. Edouart]  Newspaper clipping of silhouette of Sir Walter Scott, aged fifty-nine, writing at his desk. The original silhouette of Sir Walter Scott was cut by Augustin Edouart in Edinburgh in 1831. It is now held by the Scottish National Portrait Gallery. The Scotsman, 27 October 1928
