Galileo: From a picture by Ramsay in Trinity College, Cambridge Steel engraved portrait of Galileo by R. Hart after a painting by Ramsay Engraved portrait of the Italian astronomer Galileo Galilei (1564-1642). In May 1832 Sir Walter Scott visited the Basilica di Santa Croce in Florence where he would have seen Galileo's tomb. The painting by Ramsay is copied from an original portrait by Justus Sustermans in the Palazzo Pitti, Florence. The Portrait Gallery of Distinguished Poets, vol. I. London: Wm. S. Orr & Co., 1853 Basilica di Santa Croce Florence
