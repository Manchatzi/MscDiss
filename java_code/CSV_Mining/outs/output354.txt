The Dining Room at Abbotsford Coloured photograph of a watercolour by W. Allan of the dining room at Abbotsford Depicts the dining room of Sir Walter Scott's home of Abbotsford, Scottish Borders, on the day of the writer's death, 21 September 1832. Sir William Allan had been invited by Scott's son-in-law and future biographer J. G. Lockhart to make images of the interior apartments of Abbotsford as occupied by Scott.    Abbotsford
