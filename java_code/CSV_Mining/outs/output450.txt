Robert Boyle: From an Original Picture in the Possession of Lord Dover Steel engraved portrait of Robert Boyle by R. Woodman Engraved portrait of the English natural philosopher and chemist Robert Boyle (1626-1691). In his Life of John Dryden (1808), Sir Walter Scott discussed Dryden's passion for science and his praise of Boyle in 'Epistle to Dr Walter Charleton'. Boyle's belief in second sight is also mentioned in a note to Scott's The Lady of the Lake (1810). The original portrait by Johann Kerseboom was painted ca. 1689. The Portrait Gallery of Distinguished Poets, vol. II. London: Wm. S. Orr & Co., 1853
