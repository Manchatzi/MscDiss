Swift: From the Picture, in the Bodleian Library, Oxford Engraved portrait of Jonathan Swift by B. Holl after C. Jervas Engraved portrait of Irish satirist Jonathan Swift (1667-1745). Walter Scott published his 19-volume edition of The Works of Jonathan Swift in 1814, incorporating a lengthy biography of the author. Scott quoted frequently from Swift's works and used his Drapier's Letters (1724) as a model for Letters of Malachi Malagrowther (1826). The original portrait of Swift by Charles Jervas was painted 1709-1710. The Portrait Gallery of Distinguished Poets, vol. II. London: Wm. S. Orr & Co., 1853
