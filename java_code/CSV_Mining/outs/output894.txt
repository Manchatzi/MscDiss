Newark Castle Lithograph of Newark Castle after T. Picken after G. Cattermole Depicts Sir Walter Scott (seated) and William Wordsworth by the River Yarrow, Scottish Borders, with Newark Castle in the background. This image depicts a visit made by Scott and Wordsworth in September 1831 which inspired Wordsworth's poem 'Yarrow Revisited'. In Scott's The Lay of the Last Minstrel (1805), the minstrel receives hospitality from the Duchess of Buccleuch at Newark Castle and, in return, recites a tale about her ancestors. Lawson, J.P. Scotland Delineated: In a Series of Views by Clarkson Stanfield. London: J. Hogarth, 1847-1854   Newark Castle
