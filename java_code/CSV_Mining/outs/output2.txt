The Abbot: Lochleven Castle Steel engraving by E.F. Finden of a scene relating to Scott's novel The Abbot Depicts Loch Leven Castle, Fife, scene of Mary Stuart's imprisonment. The plot of Sir Walter Scott's The Abbot (1820) revolves around Mary's escape from the castle. The image was presumably engraved by Finden after his own design. The Waverley Album. London: Charles Heath, 1832 (facing p. 32)   Loch Leven Castle
