Canterbury: From St. Stephen's Road Engraving of Canterbury Cathedral by E. Francis after W. Westall Depicts Canterbury Cathedral, Kent, with grazing cattle in the foreground. Scott's son Walter was stationed in Canterbury as a Captain in the King's Hussars between 1827 and 1828. There are also brief references to Canterbury in Rob Roy, The Talisman, The Betrothed, and Count Robert of Paris. Moule, T. The Landscape Album, or, Great Britain Illustrated: In a Series of Sixty Views. London: Charles Tilt. 1832.   Canterbury Cathedral
