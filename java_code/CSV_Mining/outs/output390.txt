[Replica bust of Scott after the original by F.L. Chantrey]  Plaster replica of a marble bust of Scott by Sir Francis Legatt Chantrey. Scott sat for the original plaster bust by Sir Francis Legatt Chantrey in spring 1820. This plaster copy is based on the marble replica (known as the 'Abbotsford Bust') that Chantrey made for Scott in 1821.
