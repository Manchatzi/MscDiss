Alice Bridgenorth Coloured lithograph after a painting by M. O'Connor of a character in Scott's novel Peveril of the Peak Depicts Alice Bridgenorth trying to persuade her father that she has met her lover Julian by chance (Peveril of the Peak, ch. 13) The lithograph was made by M. O'Connor from his own design. Scott Gallery of Portraits (The), no. 32. London: I. Pewtrees: Ackermann & Co, 1833    Popish Plot, 1678
