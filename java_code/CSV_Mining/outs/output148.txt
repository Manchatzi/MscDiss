The Black Knight & the Clerk of Copmanhurst = Le chevalier noir et le Clerc de Copmanhurst. Engraving by George Cruikshank of a scene from Scott's novel Ivanhoe Depicts Friar Tuck and the Black Knight (King Richard in disguise) carousing in the hermitage (Ivanhoe, ch. 18). Landscape-Historical Illustrations of Scotland and the Waverley Novels, vol. I. London: Fisher, Son & Co., 1836-38 (facing p. 36)
