Newark Castle, Yarrow Facsimile of a pencil drawing of Newark Castle by G. Home Depicts Newark Castle, Scottish Borders, and surrounding countryside. In Sir Walter Scott's The Lay of the Last Minstrel (1805), the minstrel receives hospitality from the Duchess of Buccleuch at Newark Castle and, in return, recites a tale about her ancestors. Scott accompanied William Wordsworth on a visit to the ruined 15th-century castle in September 1831 which inspired Wordsworth's poem 'Yarrow Revisited'. Baikie, J. The Charm of the Scott Country.
