Caerlaverock Castle Engraved picture of Caerlaverock Castle by unknown artist Depicts the ruins of Caerlaverock Castle, Dumfriesshire. Ellangowan Castle, seat of the Bertrams in Sir Walter Scott's Guy Mannering (1815), is often thought to be based on Caerlaverock Castle which Scott visited with Guthrie Wright in 1806. The engraver may be John Landseer who signed the frontispieces to The Beauties of Scotland (presumably working from his own design). Forsyth, R. The Beauties of Scotland. Edinburgh: Printed for Thomson Bonar and John Brown, 1805   Caerlaverock Castle
