In the Market-Place, Melrose Original watercolour of Market Place, Melrose Original watercolour depicting the Market Place in Melrose, Scottish Borders. Artist unknown; dated 12 August 1848. Melrose is set in the heart of the 'Scott Country', and is featured in many of his works, most notably The Lay of the Last Minstrel (1805) and, under the guise of 'Kennaquhair', in The Monastery (1820). Town and abbey are described in detail in Scott's The Border Antiquities of England and Scotland (1814).    Melrose
