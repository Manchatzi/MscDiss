[Handwritten title page of Robert Pitcairn's copy of Scott's Letters on Demonology and Witchcraft]  Title page of Robert Pitcairn's copy of Scott's Letters on Demonology and Witchcraft, with title written in Pitcairn's own hand. The Scottish antiquary and lawyer Robert Pitcairn is best known as the author of Ancient Criminal Trials in Scotland (1833). He was an original member of the Bannatyne Club founded by Scott in 1823 to print rare works of Scottish interest. Scott, W. Letters on Demonology and Witchcraft. London: John Murray, 1830
