Marmion Engraving by F. Engleheart from a drawing by R. Westall illustrating a scene from Scott's poem Marmion Depicts Sir Ralph de Wilton in the guise of a palmer confronting Marmion (Marmion, canto I, stanza 28). Illustrates canto I, stanza 28: 'For no saluting did he wait, | But strode across the hall of state, | And fronted Marmion where he sate'. Scott, W. Marmion: Illustrated with Engravings from the Designs of Richard Westall. London: John Sharpe, 1809 (facing p. 50)    Flodden, Battle of, England, 1513
