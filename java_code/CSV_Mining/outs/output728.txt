Entrance to the Peak Cavern, Derbyshire Engraving of Peak Cavern by E. Finden after W. Westall Depicts cottages within the entrance of Peak Cavern, Derbyshire, and villagers making ropes. The hero of Sir Walter Scott's novel, Peveril of the Peak, is the descendant of the Norman knight William Peveril who built a castle overhanging the Peak Cavern or Devil's Cavern in Derbyshire. Peak Cavern has the largest natural cave entrance in the British Isles. A village was established within the entrance whose inhabitants made ropes for the local lead mines. Moule, T.
