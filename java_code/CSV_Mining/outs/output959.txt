Mickle Gate, York Engraved title page and frontispiece of Scott's Ivanhoe Title page of Ivanhoe, volume XVI of a set of the Waverley Novels published by A. & C. Black, featuring Micklegate Bar, York. Sir Walter Scott's Ivanhoe (1819) is set in Yorkshire. The Jewish financier Isaac and his daughter Rebecca are both natives of the county-town York. Micklegate (or 'great street') was the principal thoroughfare of medieval York. The original wood engraving was made for the Abbotsford edition of the Waverley Novels (1842-1847). Scott, W. Ivanhoe. Edinburgh: Adam and Charles Black, [185-?]   York
