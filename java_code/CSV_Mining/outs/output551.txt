Sir Walter Scott, Ob. 1832 Portrait of Sir Walter Scott engraved by H.T. Ryall after J.P. Knight Half-length portrait of Sir Walter Scott at the age of fifty-four. Scott sat for the original portrait by John Prescott Knight in January 1826. It was destroyed by fire in 1832. The engraving by Ryall was first published on June 1, 1833 by Harding & Lepard of London. It was subsequently reprinted in numerous publications. It is not known from which this engraving was extracted.
