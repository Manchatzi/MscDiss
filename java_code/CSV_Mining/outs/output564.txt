Literary Anniversaries 1971 Photograph of R. Dease's designs for stamps of John Keats, Thomas Gray and Sir Walter Scott Design for stamps with profile portraits of John Keats (3p), Thomas Gray (5p) and Sir Walter Scott (7 1/2p) The three stamps were designed by Rosalind Dease to commemorate the 150th anniversary of the death of John Keats, the 200th anniversary of the death of Thomas Gray and the 200th anniversary of the birth of Sir Walter Scott. The stamps were first issued on July 28, 1971. Dease's design is freely inspired by the bust by Sir Francis Legatt Chantrey.
