[Engraved profile portrait of Sir Walter Scott by A. Collas derived from a bust by F. Chantrey]  Profile portrait of Sir Walter Scott aged around fifty with facsimile of signature. Scott first sat for the original plaster bust by Sir Francis Legatt Chantrey in spring 1820. This engraving is from the marble replica (known as the 'Abbotsford Bust') that Chantrey made for Scott in 1821.This print was engraved by Achille Collas from a medallion by Henry Weekes derived from Chantrey's statue. It was first published in 1838 in The Authors of England (London: Charles Tilt). The Complete Works of Robert Burns and the Poetical Works of Sir Walter Scott. Vol. II. Glasgow: William Collins. 1867 (front.)
