Lay of the Last Minstrel Steel engraving by C. Heath from a drawing by R. Westall of a scene from Scott's poem The Lay of the Last Minstrel Depicts Tinlinn telling the Lady of Branksome Hall of the approach of the English army (Lay of the Last Minstrel, canto IV, stanza 6). Illustrates canto IV, stanza 6, lines 1-3: 'Thus to the ladye did Tinlinn shew | The Tidings of the English foe:- | Belted Will Howard is marching here'. Scott, W. The Lay of the Last Minstrel: Illustrated with Engravings from the Designs of Richd. Westall. London: John Sharpe, 1809 (facing p. 104)
