Back Stairs, Parliament House, Edin., 1817 Original coloured pencil drawing by James Skene of the back stairs, Parliament House, Edinburgh Handcoloured pencil sketch of Parliament House in Edinburgh, seat of the Faculty of Advocates, as featured in Scott's novel Redgauntlet. This drawing was subsequently etched for Skene's A Series of Sketches of the Existing Localities of the Waverley Novels (Edinburgh: Robert Cadell, 1829).  Edinburgh Parliament House
