The Abbot Engraved title page for The Abbot by W.H. Watt after E. Landseer Engraved title page for The Abbot, vol. XX in the Magnum Opus Edition of the Waverley Novels. The illustration depicts the rescue of the hero Roland Graeme by Lady Avenel's stag-hound Wolf. The inscription reads: 'Wolf marked the object of her anxiety, he swam straight to the spot where his assistance was so much wanted, and, seizing the childs under dress in his mouth, he not only kept him afloat, but towed him towards the causeway.' (ch. 1) Scott, W. Waverley Novels, vol. XX. Edinburgh: Cadell & Co., 1831
