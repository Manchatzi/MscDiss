Sir Walter Scott Bart. Engraved portrait of Walter Scott by W.H. Lizars after W. Wilkie Engraved portrait of Walter Scott at the age of sixty. The original portrait by Captain William Wilkie was painted in 1831. The undated engraving by Lizars was published by George Tait, Haddington, and Alexander Hill, Edinburgh. George Tait died in 1844.
