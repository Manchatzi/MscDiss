Glencoe Engraving of Glencoe by W.H. Lizars after J.B. Kidd Depicts Glen Coe, Scottish Highlands, under a stormy sky. Sir Walter Scott wrote the poem 'On the Massacre of Glencoe' in 1814. He also described the massacre at length in Tales of a Grandfather, Second Series (1828). Chambers, R. The Picture of Scotland. Vol. II. Edinburgh: W.
