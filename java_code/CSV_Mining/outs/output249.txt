Holm Peel Castle: Fenella & Julian Peveril = ChÌ¢teau de Holm Peel: Fenella et Julian Peveril Steel engraving by F. W. Topham of a scene from Scott's novel Peveril of the Peak Depicts Peel Castle, Isle of Man, with Peveril and Fenella slipping away in a boat (Peveril of the Peak, ch. 19). The image was both painted and engraved by F. W. Topham. Landscape-Historical Illustrations of Scotland and the Waverley Novels, vol. I. London: Fisher, Son & Co., 1836-38 (facing p. 10) Isle of Man Peel Castle  Popish Plot, 1678
