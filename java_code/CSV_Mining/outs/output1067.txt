A Legend of Montrose Engraved title page for A Legend of Montrose by F. Bacon after W. Boxall Engraved title page for A Legend of Montrose, vol. XV in the Magnum Opus Edition of the Waverley Novels. The illustration depicts the Earl of Menteith and Allan M'Aulay rescuing the heroine Annot Lyle from the Children of the Mist. The inscription reads: 'One little maiden alone, who smiled upon Allan's drawn dirk, escaped his vengeance upon my earnest entreaty. She was brought to the castle and here bred up under the name of Annot Lyle.' (ch. 5) Scott, W. Waverley Novels, vol XV. Edinburgh: Cadell & Co., 1830
