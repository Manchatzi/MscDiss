[Pencil drawing by an unknown artist from a bust of Sir Walter Scott by Sir F. L. Chantrey]  Pencil drawing of Sir Francis Legatt Chantrey's bust of Sir Walter Scott. Scott first sat for the original plaster bust by Sir Francis Legatt Chantrey in spring 1820. This drawing is of the marble replica (known as the 'Abbotsford Bust') that Chantrey made for Scott in 1821. A signature beginning with 'E' is visible to the bottom left of the sketch.
