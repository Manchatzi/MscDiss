Sir Walter Scott: From the Statue in the Monument at Edinburgh Lithograph of Sir J. Steell's statue of Sir Walter Scott by J. Sutcliffe Depicts a profile view of the statue of Walter Scott by Sir John Steell in the Scott Monument, Edinburgh. George Meikle Kemp won the competition to design a monument to Sir Walter Scott in 1838. The structure was begun in 1840 and completed in 1844. The monument, including the statue by Sir John Steell, was officially inaugurated 15 August 1846.  Edinburgh Scott Monument
