Queen Mary beside the Yew-Tree at Crookston Castle Woodcut engraving by B. Shaw of a scene from Scott's novel The Abbot Depicts Mary Queen of Scots by a yew tree with Roland Graeme behind her (Abbot, ch. 37). In The Abbot, the yew-tree marks a vantage point from which Mary observes the defeat of her followers at the Battle of Langside. Scott, W. The Abbot. Holyrood Edition.
