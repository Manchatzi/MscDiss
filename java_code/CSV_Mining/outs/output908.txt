[Covent Garden Theatre playbill for Peveril of the Peak, a musical drama adapted from Sir Walter Scott's novel]  Playbill for Peveril of the Peak; also advertises The Vauxhall Gala and Mother Shipton, among other productions. Peveril of the Peak was a musical drama adapted from Sir Walter Scott's 1823 novel with words by Isaac Pocock (1783-1835) and music by Charles Edward Horn (1786-1849). It was first performed on 17 October 1826 at Covent Garden.
