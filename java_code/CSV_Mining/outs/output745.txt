Sheffield Engraving of the city of Sheffield by E. Finden after W. Westall Depicts the River Don with the city of Sheffield, South Yorkshire, in the background. Sir Walter Scott's Ivanhoe (1819) is largely set in a tract of forest lying between the cities of Doncaster and Sheffield in South Yorkshire. The disguised hero accompanies the Jewish financier Isaac of York as far as Sheffield in chapter 6 of the novel. Scott passed through Sheffield when returning to Edinburgh from London in May 1807. His son Walter was stationed there in 1830. Moule, T. The Landscape Album, or, Great Britain Illustrated: In a Series of Sixty Views. London: Charles Tilt, 1832   Sheffield
