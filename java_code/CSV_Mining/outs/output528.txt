Marmion Engraving by J. Heath from a drawing by R. Westall illustrating a scene from Scott's poem Marmion Depicts the dying Marmion at Flodden Field, attended by Clara de Clare and a monk (Marmion, canto VI, stanza 32). Illustrates canto VI, stanza 32, lines 28-29: 'Charge, Chester, charge! On, Stanley, on!' | Were the last words of Marmion.' Scott, W. Marmion: Illustrated with Engravings from the Designs of Richard Westall. London: John Sharpe, 1809 (facing p. 366)    Flodden, Battle of, England, 1513
