Lay of the Last Minstrel Steel engraving by C. Heath from a drawing by R. Westall of a scene from Scott's poem The Lay of the Last Minstrel Depicts the Lady of Branksome Hall reunited with her rescued son, with Lord Cranstoun kneeling at her feet (Lay of the Last Minstrel, canto V, stanza 25). Illustrates canto V, stanza 25, lines 5-6: 'Yet not Lord Cranstoun deigned she greet, | Though low he kneeled at her feet'. Scott, W. The Lay of the Last Minstrel: Illustrated with Engravings from the Designs of Richd. Westall. London: John Sharpe, 1809 (facing p. 164)
