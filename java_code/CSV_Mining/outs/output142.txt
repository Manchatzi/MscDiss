Rebecca Steel engraving by Charles Heath after a drawing by C. R. Leslie of a scene from Scott's novel Ivanhoe Depicts Rebecca, heroine of Scott's Ivanhoe, imprisoned in Torquilstone Castle. Illustrates an anonymous poem entitled 'Rebecca' which imagines Rebecca's thoughts when imprisoned in Torquilstone Castle. The Keepsake for MDCCCXXVIII. London: T. Hurst & Co., 1828 (facing p. 216)
