Louis of France and the Count de Crevecoeur = Louis de France et le Comte de Crevecoeur Steel engraving by G. Presbury after a drawing by T. Allom of a scene from Scott's novel Quentin Durward Depicts the Duke of Burgundy's envoy, the Count de Crevecoeur, throwing down a gauntlet to challenge King Louis XI of France (Quentin Durward, ch. 8). Landscape-Historical Illustrations of Scotland and the Waverley Novels, vol. I. London: Fisher, Son & Co., 1836-38 (facing p. 49)
