Clara Mowbray Coloured lithograph after a painting by M. O'Connor of a character in Scott's novel St. Ronan's Well Depicts Clara Mowbray, heroine of Scott's novel St Ronan's Well. The lithograph was made by M. O'Connor from his own design. Scott Gallery of Portraits (The), no. 36. London: I. Pewtrees: Ackermann & Co, 1833
