Mrs J.G. Lockhart, Elder Daughter of Sir Walter Scott Engraved portrait of Charlotte Sophia Scott after W. Nicholson Portrait of Walter Scott's older daughter Charlotte Sophia (1799-1837) at the age of nineteen in Norwegian peasant dress. Charlotte Sophia Scott married Scott's future biographer John Gibson Lockhart on 29 April 1820. The original portrait by William Nicholson was painted in 1818. Lockhart, J.G. Memoirs of the Life of Sir Walter Scott, Bart. Edinburgh: A. & C. Black, 1871 (facing p. 428)
