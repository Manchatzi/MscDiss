Jokeby: A Burlesque on Rokeby; A Poem, in Six Cantos Title page and coloured frontispiece by Richardson of Jokeby, a Burlesque on Rokeby Frontispiece illustration and title page of a parody of Sir Walter Scott's poem Rokeby written by John Roby. The frontispiece illustration was most probably etched by Rowlandson from his own design and then aquatinted by an anonymous professional engraver. Roby, J. Jokeby, a Burlesque on Rokeby: A Poem, in six Cantos. By an amateur of fashion. London: Thomas Tegg, 1813 (frontispiece)
