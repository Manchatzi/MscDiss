Fast Castle Engraving by Edward Finden after a drawing by Copley Fielding of a scene relating to Scott's novel The Bride of Lammermoor Depicts Fast Castle, Berwickshire, the reputed model for the Ravenswood family's castle of Wolf's Crag in Scott's novel The Bride of Lammermoor.
