King Engraved portrait of the English horologist John Harrison. The English horologist John Harrison is best known for inventing the first practical marine chronometer, enabling navigators to compute accurately their longitude at sea. This engraving by William Holl is based on a previous engraving by Philippe Joseph Tassaert. The original portrait by Thomas King was painted in 1767. The Portrait Gallery of Distinguished Poets, vol. II. London: Wm. S. Orr & Co., 1853
