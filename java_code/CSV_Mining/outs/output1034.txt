[Facsimile of silhouette of Sir Walter Scott by A. Edouart]  Profile full-length silhouette of Sir Walter Scott at the age of fifty-nine (with slight water damage). The original silhouette of Sir Walter Scott was cut by Augustin Edouart in Edinburgh in 1831. It is now held by the Scottish National Portrait Gallery. The Connoisseur, 90 (Sept. 1932), p.169
