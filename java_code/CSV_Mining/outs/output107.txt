David Dean's [sic] Cottage Steel vignette engraving by W. H. Lizars of a scene from Scott's novel The Heart of Midlothian Title page vignette depicting David Deans's cottage in St Leonard's, Edinburgh, with the Salisbury Crags and Arthur's Seat in the background. The image was engraved by Lizars after his own design. Dramas from the Novels, Tales and Romances of the Author of Waverley, vol. I. Edinburgh: James L. Huie, 1823 Arthur's Seat Edinburgh
