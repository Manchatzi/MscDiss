Loch Katrine Engraving of Loch Katrine by W.H. Lizars after J.B. Kidd Depicts Loch Katrine, Stirling, with Ben Venue in the background. Loch Katrine provides the setting for Sir Walter Scott's The Lady of the Lake (1810). Following the success of the poem, the Loch and the surrounding Trossachs region became major tourist attractions. Chambers, R. The Picture of Scotland. Vol. II. Edinburgh: W.
