Lucy Ashton Title page and frontispiece by A.H. Payne of a German translation of Scott's novel The Bride of Lammermoor Portrait of Lucy Ashton, the heroine of Sir Walter Scott's novel The Bride of Lammermoor. Faces title-page of a German translation of the novel. The engraving is by Albert Henry Payne, a German-based English artist, presumably from his own design. Scott, W. Die Braut von Lammermoor (tr. Wilhelm Sauerwein). Stuttgart: Hoffmann'sche Verlagsbuchhandlung, 1851 (frontispiece)
