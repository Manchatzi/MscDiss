Lord President Hope, 1763-1851 Photogravure of a portrait of Charles Hope, Lord Granton by J. W. Gordon Portrait of Charles Hope of Granton (1763-1851) as Lord President of the Court of Session. Charles Hope, Lord Granton was Lord Justice Clerk (1804-1811) and later Lord President of the Court of Session (1811-1841) while Sir Walter Scott was Clerk of Session. The Hopes were also social acquaintances of the Scotts. The original portrait was painted in 1832 by John Watson Gordon. The History of the Speculative Society, 1764-1904. Edinburgh: Printed for the Society by T. and A. Constable, 1905 (facing p. 96)
