Marmion Engraving by J. Mitan from a drawing by R. Westall illustrating a scene from Scott's poem Marmion Depicts King James IV listening to Lady Heron sing 'Lochinvar' (Marmion, canto V, stanza 12). Illustrates canto V, stanza 12, lines 1-2: 'The monarch o'er the siren hung, | And beat the measure as she sung'. Scott, W. Marmion: Illustrated with Engravings from the Designs of Richard Westall. London: John Sharpe, 1809 (facing p. 262)    Flodden, Battle of, England, 1513
