Brindley: From a Picture by R. Dunkarton 1773, after a Picture by F. Parsons Steel engraved portrait of James Brindley by J. T. Wedgwood after a picture by F. Parsons Engraved portrait of the English engineer James Brindley (1716-1772). The original portrait by Francis Parsons was painted in 1770. Wedgwood's engraving is taken from a mezzotint by Dunkarton printed in 1773. The Portrait Gallery of Distinguished Poets, vol. III. London: Wm. S. Orr & Co., 1853
