[Lithograph of the Battle of Prestonpans]  Depicts the death of the Hanoverian Colonel Gardiner at the hands of the Jacobites in the Battle of Prestonpans (featured in Waverley, ch. 47). The original painting was exhibited in 1842. Lawson, J.P. Scotland delineated in a Series of Views, vol. I. London: E.
