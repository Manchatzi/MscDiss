St Mary's Steel engraving by E.F. Finden after a painting by S. Prout relating to Scott's novel The Abbot Depicts the ruins of Melrose Abbey, Scottish Borders, portrayed as the Monastery of Kennaquhair in the Abbot. Melrose Abbey also appears in The Lay of the Last Minstrel (1805), The Monastery (1820), and is described in Scott's 'Essay on Border Antiquities (1814). Landscape Illustrations of the Waverley Novels: With Descriptions of the Views, vol. II. London: Charles Tilt, 1832, no. 46   Melrose Abbey
