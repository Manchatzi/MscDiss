Wolf's Hope = Port de Wolf's Hope Steel engraving by J. H. Kernot after a drawing by J. M. W. Turner of a scene from Scott's novel The Bride of Lammermoor Depicts Caleb Balderstone leaving Wolf's Hope (based on Eyemouth, Berwickshire) after a visit to Gibbie Girder (Bride of Lammermoor, ch. 18). Landscape-Historical Illustrations of Scotland and the Waverley Novels, vol. II. London: Fisher, Son & Co., 1836-38 (facing p. 79)   Eyemouth
