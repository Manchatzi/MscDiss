Cathedral & Bishop's Palace of Kirkwall Original pencil drawing by James Skene of Kirkwall Cathedral and the Bishop's Palace Original pencil drawing of St Magnus Cathedral and the Bishop's Palace in Kirkwall, Orkney, both described in The Pirate, ch. 31. This drawing was subsequently etched for Skene's A Series of Sketches of the Existing Localities of the Waverley Novels (Edinburgh: Robert Cadell, 1829).  Kirkwall St. Magnus Cathedral
