Tolbooth Steel engraving by Edward Finden after a drawing by A. Naysmith of a scene from Scott's novel The Heart of Midlothian Depicts the Old Tolbooth or town prison of Edinburgh (commonly known as the 'Heart of Midlothian'), scene of Effie Deans's imprisonment.
