Ben Lomond from above Luss, Dumbarton-shire Engraving of Ben Lomond by J. Swan after J. Fleming Depicts a view of Loch Lomond with Ben Lomond in the background. The dramatic scenery of Loch Lomond and Ben Lomond feature prominently in both The Lady of the Lake and Rob Roy. While researching Rob Roy in July 1817, Scott visited the outlaw's cave at the head of Loch Lomond. Leighton, J.M. The Lakes of Scotland: A Series of Views. Glasgow: J. Swan. 1834 (t.p.)
