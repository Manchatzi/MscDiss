The Talisman Engraved title page for The Talisman by S. Sangster after J.W. Gordon Engraved title page for The Talisman, vol. XXXVIII in the Magnum Opus Edition of the Waverley Novels. The illustration portrays the meeting between the novel's hero, Sir Kenneth and Saladin. The inscription reads: 'Christian and Saracen, sat down together on the turf, and produced each the small allowance of store which they carried for their own refreshment.' (ch. 2) Scott, W. Waverley Novels, vol. XXXVIII. Edinburgh: Cadell & Co., 1832    Crusades - Third, 1189-1192
