'We're proud to see ye, Shirra. Would ye honour us by comin' ben?' Facsimile of a drawing depicting a meeting between Sir Walter Scott and Tom Purdie Depicts Tom Purdie inviting Scott, whom he addresses as Sheriff Deputy of Selkirkshire, into his home. 'A Friend in Need' by Halbert Johnston Boyd is a fictionalized account of the first meeting between Scott and his retainer Tom Purdie ca. 1804 following a poaching incident. The Romance of Scott: His Home, His Work, His Country; Sir Walter Scott Centenary, 1832-1932. Edinburgh: Travel Press and Publicity Co., 1932 (p. 53)
